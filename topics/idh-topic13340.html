Subject: UTAC NS-7000 output shuttle side ION blower fan 5802i faulty

Handler Model: NS-7000 (NS70-21, S/N: 18980)

Controller: RC520

Date: 14 Sep 2010


 

Symptom

Blower fan unable to adjust balance. Red LED light always ON.

Low speed balance is +70, high speed balance is +150.


 


 

Action

Turned balance adjustment knob clockwise and counter clockwise also no change.

Replace fan temporary from NS70-57, fan s/n:R11485.

Raise warranty S0448 for NS70-21 fan s/n:09522.

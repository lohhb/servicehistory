Subject: UTAC – NS8080-03 chamber unable to heat up

Handler Model: NS-8080 (S/N: 181695)

Controller: RC520 (S/N: 03-00596)

Date: 11 Aug 2011


 

Symptom

UTAC – NS8080-03 chamber unable to heat up.


 


 

Action

- Checked chamber heater thermo fuse no resistance.

- Replaced 192 deg thermo fuse with Getech spare.

- Heat up to 50 deg and 70 deg ok.

- Released handler to KK Chia.

- Warranty report S0498 raised.


 


 

Cause

Thermo fuse open circuit(blown).

Service History

Subject: NS-7080 Index arm tubing Replacement

Handler Model: NS-7080 (Amkor Singapore)

Controller: RC520

Date: 31 Apr 2008


 

Symptom


 

Action

Index Arm Tubing change. 9 tubings per Index Arm.

Old tubing for Index Arm removal.

Re-condition of spacer and fitting with loctite.

New tubing cut to length + 20mm for allowance.

Greasing of new tubing.

Insertion of new tubing.

Tighten of top for all tubing.

Check max/min stroke with appropriate slack.

Tighten bottom to appropriate length.

Repeat for other arm. Verify tubing channeling with I/O monitor.

Total 6.5 hrs

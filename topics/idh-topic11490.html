Subject: NS-8040 Input arm crash issue

Handler Model: NS-8040 (NS80-26, S/N: 181540)

Controller: RC520

Date: 11 Jan 2010


 

Symptom

UTAC has one NS-8040 they claimed input arm crashed and damaged the Input hand B and D, causing floating issues on the input shuttle and smashed devices when undetected floating units were picked up by the index arm.

 

Today they wanted us to replace the hands with NS80-20(spare part handler in PM room), assess the damage and raise warranty . After we(Hidaka san, Melvin and myself) checked the handler, we found some shim(paper) inserted into hand B and D for alignment after the crash. We removed the shims, checked the hands physically for any bending and tighten back the hands in position. We also checked the hand pitches with shuttle alignment jig and concluded the hands in good condition.

 

However, the input arm main X axis was not in position. We also found hit marks at the joint area. We are unsure how the arm could have crashed but the index door and door sensor bracket were damaged too.

 

We need advise now on how to calibrate the X axis back in position and I believe Hidaka san already sent email to sub-con.

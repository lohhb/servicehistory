Service History

Subject: UTAC NS8080-03 shuttle position auto shift during running, Input hand up sensor error

Handler Model: NS-8080 (NS8080-03) 8 Hands High UPH

Controller: RC520

Date: 26 Jan 2011


 

Symptom

1. Advised by Hidaka san, the shifting motion of the shuttles when near to loading/unloading position is cater for shuttle expansion in hot mode. The origin sensor detect the shuttle position every 5 mins before the shuttle reach the actual position. It then automatically calculate and correct the offset position.

However, ambient mode will also have this motion. But it will not affect UPH.


 

2. NS8080-03 again encountered hand up sensor for the input arm this morning. According to Giovanni, he used his finger to press onto the connectors on the distribution boards and handler able to run without the error again. Feedback to Hidaka san and sent event log.

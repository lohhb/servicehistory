Subject: Infineon enquire on flexibility of NS-8000 kit on NS-6000 handler

Handler Model: NS-8040

Controller: Rc520

Date: 24 Aug 2009


 

Symptom

Infineon enquire on flexibility of NS-8000 kit on NS-6000 handler.


 

Change kit info:

NS8040 LF2BGA189 change kit

Dual Site

60mm pitch

NHK socket


 

There is no issue to share any NS-6040 change kit to NS-8040 handler but there is some constraints to share NS8040 change kit to NS6040 handler.


 

So,

The LF2BGA189 cannot share with NS-6040 handler because:

1.         The NS-6040 shuttle sensor x-pitch position FIXED at 40mm; 80mm and 120mm.

            There is no 60mm x-pitch sensor.

2.         There are 2 types of LF2BGA189 dock plate we did before:

            1 caters for NS6040 mounting and 1 caters for NS7000 mounting.

            The latest dock plate using now is the one cater for NS7000 mounting.


 

So, there is recommend to share the LF2BGA189 change kit with NS-7000 handler with retrofit kit because:

1.         The NS7000 shuttle sensor x-pitch position is adjustable.

2.         The latest dock plate is caters for NS-7000 mounting.

Service History

Subject: No 24V for Input Shutter sensor

Handler Model: NS-7080 (Xilinx, S/N: 121266)

Controller: RC520

Date: 20 Mar 2008


 

Symptom

Transmitter found to be chaffed by something, most probably by input shutter (notice quite a bit of free play on the wire, already informed Xilinx to take note of this, that is, to cable tie all sensor wire after they make any adjustment to the sensor). 


 

Action

Transmitter replaced and check carried out on the transmitter found good.  Production resumed after replacement of transmitter and everything is good.

Warranty S0293 raised.


 


 

Cause

Input shutter sensor wire broken due to rubbing by Input Shuttle.

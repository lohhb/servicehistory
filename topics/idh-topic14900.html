XAP – NS7KW-06 Output arm hand C and D pitching out.Actually, before that Output Hand B crashed and customer did replacement.

–     Aligned Hand C and D.

–     Verified placement of Output Arm Hand on Auto 1 and 2, Fix 1 and Automats.

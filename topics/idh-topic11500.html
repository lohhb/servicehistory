Subject: NS-8040 175DegC Socket Air 2 temp drop and raise during the dynamic temp verification

Handler Model: NS-8040

Controller: RC520

Date: 14 Jan 2010


 

Symptom

Docking up of NS8040 with J950 Tester. Dynamic temperature verification and adjustment carried out on 160 deg for 10sec and 6mins.

Socket Air 2 temp drop and raise during the dynamic temp verification.


 


 

Action

Checking using Omron software confirm socket air 2 got problem.

Resistance check on PT sensor, heater and TC found without any abnormalities.

After resit connectors, temperature was heat up to 160 deg to continue the verification. Everything back to normal.


 


 

Cause

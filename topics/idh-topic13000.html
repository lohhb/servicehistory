Service History

Subject: STM NS-8080 High jamming in Ion balance failed sensor 3

Handler Model: NS-8080 (S/N: 181581)

Controller: RC520

Date: 20 Jul 2010


 

Symptom

High jamming in Ion balance failed sensor 3.


 


 

Action

-ESD (Decay time check)

-Ionizer balance check.

-the warning errors Ion balance failed sensor 3 still persist, found 2 pieces of discharge needle was burned.

-Swapped the discharge needle from handler 2, warning errors solved.


 


 

Cause

Worn out ionizer's discharge needles.


 

Remarks

To turn on Chamber fan, ON SPEL I/O Output Bit 272 & 273.

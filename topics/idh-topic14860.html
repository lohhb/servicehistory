Service History

Subject: SCB – NS-7160W(121046) input arm hit to tray arm issue

Handler Model: NS-7160W

Controller: RC520

Date: 21 Mar 2011


 

Symptom

SCB – NS-7160W(121046) input arm hit to tray arm issue.


 


 

Action

1. Input arm alignment problem after hit to tray arm

- Was informed by Phumipat, Mr. Chamnan already tried to adjust alignment of input arm unit.

- After handler released for checking, we used 80x60mm shuttle alignment jig to check place position at input shuttle 2. All 4 hands were out of guide holes.

- Hand B already offset –Y from guide hole at the hand adaptor and the moving plate 2 close to rubbing against top plate.

- Even after loosening all the input arm assy screws, the 4 hands were difficult to align properly to 80x60 pitching. Especially hand B will hit top plate.

- Therefore we tried to use our spare input arm assy. . After we replaced the part and fixed back the 4 old hands, we were able to align to 80x60mm pitching. However, alignment to the shuttle jig for all 4 hands were out a bit in the same orientation. As we did not have the auto teaching jig, we fine tune the input arm pick and place from the MMI offset.

- Phumipat already talked to Mr. Krengkrai on the replacement items and they will purchase them.


 

2. Index arm 1 and 2 device drop error

- As site Ba was disabled when we check the handler, we tried to enable it after we replaced and aligned the input arm assy.

- Encountered few counts of device drop error after testing on index arm 2 site Ab.

- Check index arm P50 and P51 position with the C/K ok.

- Check back handler history and production log book, drop error for arm 1 and 2 started near end Feb. However, SCB did not complain/feedback to Phumipat about it during last few visits.

- Check condition of contactor heads and socket guide pins not so good. Some of contactor head guide holes already worn or chipped and socket guide pins surface were not so smooth.

- SCB did not buy and keep any spares for either of these 2 items.

- Hotplates were from KA, the rest of the kits were from DB Design. We should recommend SCB to buy and replace worn out C/K parts for this case.

- Before we left, we asked the one of the line engineer to swap the contactor head Aa/Ab with head Ag/Ah to see if device drop error on site Ab migrate. Tomorrow morning we will check result with him again.

3. Mr Krengkrai unable to accept low uph with 1 site disabled

- As confirmed by Goto san with EPSON Japan, when 1 site disabled, pick up sequence for loader tray will be 1 by 1 instead of 2 by 2.

- We have also explained to Mr. Krengkrai but he still cannot accept it. Even if it means disable 8 site and running only 8 sites to get a higher uph for example.


 

22 Mar 2011

1. Input arm alignment problem after hit to tray arm

- No issue

- Service report(013160) acknowledged and signed by Mr. Chamnan.

- KG to send quotation for replaced parts, SCB to issue PO.


 

2. Index arm 1 and 2 device drop error

- Still encountering device drop issue for index arm. But not on site Ab after contactor head Aa/Ab swapped with head Ag/Ah on 21 Mar night.

- Error history showed random drop error on arm 1 and 2.

- At 7am, according to line engineer, he swapped arm 2 contactor head Bg/Bh with Ac/Ad due to drop error issue on site Bg. He found a small metal debris stuck inside site Bg vacuum port of arm 2 SLK. Advised SCB to take note when doing cleaning of socket and c/k, also check condition of SLK. When we checked handler at 1130am, line engineer reported no more occurrence of drop error for site Bg.

- For arm 1, started encountering site Af frequent device drop error issue. Contactor head Ae/Af was swapped with Ac/Ad.

- At 6pm, all sockets replaced with new spares.

- According to MR. Chamnan, he will contact to DB Design for the worn out contactors and socket guide pins and spare parts.


 

3. Mr Krengkrai unable to accept low uph with 1 site disabled

- Collected online production uph data.

- 16 sites uph = 4700, disabled 1 site and run 15 sites uph = 3200. 16 sites loader pick up sequence is 2x2, 15 sites loader pick up sequence is 1x1 Z-mode.

- As per Goto san request, running setup file and observer data sent. MMI version also confirm as MMI7160W-1.0K.

- Mr. Krengkrai wants timeline from Goto san, when can reply on this issue after Japan checked setup file. Also he need software to be improved for 2x2 pickup on loader tray even if 1 site disabled.

SR013160.

Service History

Subject: NS-8040 Output Arm Hand C and D low vacuum

Handler Model: NS-8040 (NS-105, S/N: 181394)

Controller: RC520

Date: 6 Jan 2010


 

Symptom

Infineon NS-8040, NS-105 s/n: 181394 Output Arm Hand C and D low vacuum.


 


 

Action

Triggered vacuum from HMI, Hand C and D vacuum reading w/o unit showing  -26KPa and

-8KPa respectively.

Checked filter gasket, both gasket not placed properly.

Checked vacuum unit gasket, found Hand C not placed properly.

Rectified problem, triggered vacuum both Hand C and D showing  -32KPa and -33KPa respectively.

Cycle run with units, found Hand C vacuum in open pitch OK but during close pitch vacuum drop. Found out air tube broken.

Trimmed air tube OK.

Checked vacuum units settings for S1 and S2 incorrect. Adjusted to factory settings.


 


 

Cause

- vacuum unit's gasket not place properly

- Hand C air tube broken.

Service History

Subject: NS7KW-02 (Xilinx) - SLK Arm 1 stuck at dock plate during height calibration

Handler Model:  NS-7080W

Controller: RC520

Date: 23 Feb 2009


 

Symptom

Upon arrival at Xilinx, this handler is running production.  The Change Kit ( C/K ) that has problem is NS7000-27, 1x3 sites, FF1156, 35x35.  After index arm 1 stuck at dock plate during height calibration, the latch at compliance unit was bent, thus, they changed it.   But the issue is still there.  Then, they changed to NS7000-31, 1x3 sites, FG352, 35x35 and was running production without error.  Both KA kits (NS7000-27 looks like a new kit).  Upon examining the contactor, saw that there is some abrasion at the guide pin area and stiffness is felt when manually moving the contactor in and out of the dock plate guide pin.  Thus, I had advised Xilinx to get KA to come in and take a look.


 


 

Action


 


 

Cause


 

Remarks

-Check diameter of guide pins on socket guide plate

-Check if guide pins bent/worn out

-Check nest guide pin's hole

-Check metal hooks of Socket Layout Kit (SLK)

-Check correct brand and specification of latch use for (Index) Test Hands

-Check metal latch worn out at Index Test Hand

-Check Index teaching position P50 and P51

-Check if Index Arm crash before or Index Arm bent(Index Arm alignment check)

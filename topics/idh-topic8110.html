Service History

Subject: During NS-7000 buyoff Device drop at Arm 1 and Arm 2.

Handler Model: NS-7000 (S/N: 18732)

Controller: RC520

Date: 19 Dec 2008


 

Symptom

During NS-7000 buyoff Device drop at Arm 1 and Arm 2.


 

Action

Dual site convert to 4-site at the test socket.

1. Encounter device drop error at arm 1 and arm 2.

Index counter air regulator unable to adjust air flow. Found arm 2A and 2D vacuum unit leakage, replaced and counter air regulator able to regulate air flow.

Also, re-adjust all index vacuum unit sensitivity, dismantled contactors and cleaned plungers and O rings.


 

2. Because incoming lots are using HWA SHU trays, many jams occurred at loading and unloading. Realigned all loaders and unloaders to solve tray jamming.


 

3. Output arm hand B unable to place device properly onto the tray pocket towards the back row. Found hand B grounding cable lug jammed by the air tubing and wiring cable.


 

Completed VM check for 4-site with half tray of passed device.


 

Completed the 3rd qual lot of 1K.


 

Pending 3 packages(48QFP 7x7, 64QFP 10x10, 100QFP14x14) correlation to complete next week. Getech to provide support, SILABS to do package conversion.


 


 

Cause

-Faulty Air regulator for Index Arm

-Vacuum leak at Index Arm 2A and 2D

-For Index contactors, need to clean plungers and ) rings.


 

Remarks

-Check diameter of guide pins on socket guide plate

-Check any broken/bent tubing or tubing pressed by cable tie on SLK

-Check air fitting/elbow or gasket not loose on SLK

-Check if guide pins bent/worn out

-Check vacuum filter dirty

-Check vacuum filter put properly

-Check seal for vacuum put properly and not torn

-Check plastic casing for vacuum filter not broken

-Check nest guide pin's hole

-Check metal hooks of Socket Layout Kit (SLK)

-Check correct brand and specification of latch use for (Index) Test Hands

-Check metal latch worn out at Index Test Hand

-Check Index teaching position P50 and P51

-Check if Index Arm crash before or Index Arm bent(Index Arm alignment check)

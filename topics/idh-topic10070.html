Service History

Subject: Auto 1 (full traywith devices) sometimes stuck and Hotplate double devices

Handler Model: NS-7080W (NS7KW-06, S/N: 121040)

Controller: RC520

Date: 17 Apr 2009


 

Symptom

Auto 1 sometimes stuck. Full tray unable to unload.

Empty tray OK. Full tray N.G most of the time.


 

Hotplate double device and input arm unable to pick up device while checking for device presence on HOTPLATE.


 

Action

Found gap of tray guide to be less than 1 ruler thickness.

Adjusted with full tray to 1 ruler thickness. No more problem.

Hotplate double device and input arm unable to pick up device while checking for device presence on HOTPLATE.

XAP complained without device pressure to be -45kpa while sensor ON at -50kpa.

Advised XAP to clean/maintenance of vacuum path as this is internal pressure build up.

Double device on hotplate maybe because the pick up position is too high or because internal pressure is close to -50kpa, can increase to about -60kpa before maintenance of vacuum line.

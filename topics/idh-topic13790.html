Subject: NS-8040 unable to communicate with tester

Handler Model: NS-8040 (NS-005, S/N: 181669)

Controller: RC520

Date: 1 Nov 2010


 

Symptom

Infineon NS-8040 NS-005 S/N: 181669, feedback by customer handler unable to communicate with Tester.

HDD recovery was previously carried out and also data restored by BackupSan.


 


 

Action

Edited Model name to NS-8040 in Product.dat in C:NS8000/log folder.

Checked Handler config to ensure after backup is to infineon's specification.


 


 

Cause

BackupSan did not restore Product.dat.


 


 

Remarks

Refer 0254-NS8K for information on NS-8000 BackupSan.


 

Product.dat, Security setting and Password were not restored by BackupSan.

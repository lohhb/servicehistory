Service History

Subject: NS80-40 ATC thermal head unable to heat up

Handler Model: NS-8040 (S/N: 181880, UTAC)

Controller: RC520 (S/N: 03-10155)

Date: 6 Jun 2011


 

Symptom

NS80-40 ATC thermal head unable to heat up.

- Chiller temperature was set at 40 deg.

- Running temperature at 110 deg. During testing, temperature will drop to 105 deg and unable to recover.

- At noon time, temperature suddenly dropped and unable to heat up again.


 


 

Action

- Check resistance of peltier – 150K ohms.

- Received thermal grease from Roel, top up chiller water, clean the filter.

- Dismantle thermal head and used IPA to clean up the interior and exterior.

- Replaced new peltier with thermal grease.

- Checked no water leakage from thermal head.

- Checked chiller sp at 25 deg, heat up to 105 deg on thermal controller - Ok. Thermal controller power output at 80+%.

- Released and monitored production run.

- Old peltier seems to have crack mark on surface.


 


 

Cause

Peltier cracked on it's surface.

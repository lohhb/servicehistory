Service History

Subject: ASE after index arm tubing change, F-5041 torque error in low power state during HOME of handler

Handler Model: NS-7000 (S/N: 18857)

Controller: RC520

Date: 7 Sep 2010


 

Symptom

After index arm swivel air fitting and tubing change, Encounter  F-5041 Torque error in the low power state manipulator :1 joint:2 error.

Calibration but same error.


 


 

Action

Servo driver swapped with z-axis. Same error.

Solid State Relay SSR should be ok as e_tray, z and u axis is ok.

Motor on, no torque on Index arm y-axis.

Found motor power connector loose.

Re-connect power connector and found cables to be very tight fit. Suspect during index arm tubing change, cable was tugged and power connector loose.


 


 

Cause

Motor power connector loose caused torque error in low power state during homing of NS-7000

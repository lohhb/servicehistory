Service History

Subject: Faulty Vacuum Pump

Handler Model: NS-6040 (STATS ChipPac Ltd, NS6040_113)

Controller: SRC520

Date: 11~12 Nov 2008


 

Symptom

a) UPH conducted from 0sec to 6sec and backup the system before CPU upgrading. 850MHz CPU board and MIB were installed on the SRC controller.

System BIOS was configured and Win98, MMI and SPEL were installed using CPU upgrading CD.

Upgrading of the MMI to ver C7.14-SSY and restoration of the backup files.


 

b) UPH conducted from 0sec to 6sec after CPU upgrading.

During height calibration found no vacuum at the index arm.

Found that the vacuum pump faulty.

Vacuum pump was replaced by AE.

Dummy run on 14x20 carried out.

Alignment at  Auto 1-3 and Fix 1-3 out therefore have to re-align.


 


 

Action


 


 

Cause

Faulty vacuum pump for Index Arm.

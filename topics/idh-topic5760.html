Subject: DU1, DU2, DU3 have Error LED turn on at Drive Unit

Handler Model: NS7160W (NS70-02W, EEMS)

Controller: RC520

Date: 21 Feb 2008


 

Symptom

DU1, DU2 and DU3 have Error LED on at Drive Unit.

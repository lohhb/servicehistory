Service History

Subject: IDT NS7000  9x9 new change-kit buy-off

Handler Model: NS-7000(S/N: 18724)

Controller: RC520

Date: 1 Sep 2009


 

Symptom

New 5x5 change-kit buy-off no problem on 31/08/09.  New 9x9 change-kit buy-off no problem for ambient mode.  But experience a lot of flying device in index area when running hot mode.


 


 

Action

Found out that counter air on time was meddled with.  After setting the correct counter air on time, there is no flying device at all.


 


 

Cause

Service History

Subject: CPU upgrade handler reported “hang up”

Handler Model: NS-6040(ASE)

Controller: SRC520CE

Date: 19 Dec 2007


 

Symptom

Type 3 CPU upgrade for H20-60 (S/N:12731) experienced 2 cases of hang up. Monday 2am and Tues 2am.


 


 

Action

Check controller connection, tightened all connectors.

Re-install MMI.

Data/information collection to feedback to EPSON.


 


 

Cause

Hang could be due to loose connection behind controller.

Service History

Subject:

Handler Model: NS-8000

Controller:

Date: 16 & 18 May 2011


 

Symptom

1. NS8080-03 RS485 unknown error

- Encountered RS485 unknown error after input arm hand B hand up sensor error.

- Upgrade HMI from v.1.8 to v.1.9.


 

2. NS8080-02 W0422-shuttle 1 sensor error(loading side)

- Found input shuttle side input IO module connector loose.


 

3. NS80-40 ATC peltier thermal head unable to achieve 110 deg

- Checked resistance of peltier over 10 Ohms.

- Replaced with UTAC spare peltier WITHOUT thermal grease.

- Checked no water leakage from thermal head.

- Checked chiller sp at 20 deg, heat up to 110 deg on thermal controller - Ok. Thermal controller power output at 80+%.

- Released and monitored production run.

- Advised Roel to get spare thermal grease and bring down temperature of ATC thermal head when no production to prolong the lifespan of peltier.


 


 

Action

1. NS8080-03 input hand B hand up sensor error

- Last activity for hand up sensor error issue was in Jan 27(removed input arm unit assy, resit connectors).

- Checked event log, last error occurred on 17 May 814pm. Production run and end lot at 5pm no occurrence.

- Collect observer data and log folder to send to Hidaka san. To discuss the next course of action.


 

2. NS8080-02 chamber temperature unstable and input arm vacuum chattering error

- Upgrade HMI from v1.8 to v1.9.

- Original setup, I5974-5x5 hot mode 75 deg.

- According to Albert, when handler jam and arm 1 in socket, chamber temperature will fluctuate out of range.

- Installed CK 48QFN-7x7 and simulate dry cycle run. Paused handler when arm 1 in socket. Monitored chamber temperature no fluctuation. Also tried on arm 2 ok.

- Drew out dummy units and cycle run. Encountered input hand B vacuum chattering error after pick up from hotplate to shuttle.

- Checked and found air tubing at top of input arm assy not in good position. Adjusted and also removed hand B adaptor to blow clean the filter.

- Continue cycle dummy units no occurrence.


 

3. NS80-40 ATC

- Checked on ATC NS80-40 at 4.30pm and noticed thermal head temp was at 110 deg although handler was not in production since 12.30pm.

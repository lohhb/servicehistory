Warranty Parts Requisition Record

Reference No: S0288

Customer: UTAC Thailand Limited (UTL3)

Handler Model: NS-7080 (NS70-02)

Handler S/N: 181429

Mfg: Feb 2008

Controller: RC520

MMI ver: 2.0k

Date: 6 Mar 2008


 

Item requested: 400W Motor Driver Module

Part Code: R12R520DRU006


 

Symptom

UTL NS-7080 (S/N: 181429) buyoff progress for 27 Feb 08

Move handler to tester as requested by customer.

Upon pressing HOME button, abnormal noise was heard from Index Arm, MMI display error "4241 The detected speed at low power state is too fast".

Off power switch, check index area for any foreign objects, check any loose bolts from linear guides.

Check Index Arm 1 & 2 and Y movement, it was smooth. Break release switch was function normal.

Check Index motors power, signal and break connectors connection, seem ok.

Reset handler, press HOME, found that Index arm Z and U upon moving up had vibration and abnormal noise.

Check wiring connectors behind Drive Unit 1 (DU1).


 

In SPEL, selrb1 and upon Motor ON, abnormal noise heard.

Open top cover above Index motors, Motor On for selrb1, saw Z Axis timing belt strong vibration and noise from Z motor.

In SPEL, error history shows the following:

F-5041 Torque error in the low power state, Manipulator 1, Joint 3.

F-5047 Overspeed in the low power state, Manipulator 1, Joint 3.

T-4241 The detected speed at the low power state is too fast, manipulator 1, Joint 3.

F-5041 Torque error in the low power state, Manipulator 4, Joint 1.

In DU1, swapped Motor Driver1(Index Y Axis) with Motor Driver 3 (Index Z Axis).

In SPEL, Motor On, Z and U move up and error F-5047 Overspeed in the low power state, Manipulator 1, Joint 2., so the error migrated to Index Arm Y Axis.


 

In DU1, swapped the Motor Driver 3 (Index Z Axis) with Motor Driver 2(Input Arm X Axis) from DU2.

Index Arm able to HOME, no abnormal noise and no vibration, however, when Homing Input Arm, vibration was seem from Input Arm and abnormal noise.

Based on the above, the 400W Motor driver in DU1 for Index Arm Z Axis was faulty.


 


 

Remarks

Faulty 400W Motor Driver Module was replaced on 3 Mar 2008.

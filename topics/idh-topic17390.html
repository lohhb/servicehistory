Subject: NS-7000 Device cracked in socket

Handler Model: NS-7000 IDT(M)

Controller: RC520

Date: 4 Jan 2012


 

Symptom

IDT_M NS7000

1) Device crack in socket.
2) Customer feedback for NS7000, during initial start or continuous mode, test arm does not check device in socket.
3) Customer verified many times on pause+reset or jam happen and pause+reset etc, handler does not check device in socket if device stuck in socket.


 


 

Action

Chuah dispatched and found out this only happens if customer disable arm 2(shuttle 2).
Will feedback to EPSON alternative check when customer disables shuttle 2.


 


 

Cause


 


 

Remarks

Note: Even if Index Arm 2 is disabled by Shuttle selection, With Device, during or after One Cycle, Index Arm 2 will still go down to test site to check for device in socket.

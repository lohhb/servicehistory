Subject: UTAC NS70-62 device misplacement at output shuttles for BGA 3x3

Handler Model: NS-7000(NS70-62)

Controller: RC520

Date: 5 Oct 2010


 

Symptom

Flying BGA 3x3 devices and flipped device at output shuttle 1 and 2 after testing.

Error encountered "detection sensor errors and shuttle condition errors".


 


 

Action

-check arm 1 and 2 counter air flow and fine tune.

-adjust counter air regulator to 0.4mpa.

-increase counter air on time from 0.1s to 0.3s.

-perform height calibration.

-cycle run 3 trays ok.

-dock up tester and release to production.

SR:011938


 


 

Cause

Counter air volume too big.

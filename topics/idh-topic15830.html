Subject: UTAC – NS8080-01 loader tray pick up error

Handler Model: UTAC NS-8080 (NS8080-01, S/N: 181665)

Controller: RC520

Date: 11 Apr 2011


 

Symptom

UTAC – NS8080-01 loader tray pick up error.


 


 

Action

Attached dial gauge onto input arm.- Checked tolerance across L-belt guide(~0.15mm difference)- Checked tolerance across R-belt guide(~0.51mm difference)- Found middle and screw outside of handler loose, adjust flatness of R-belt guide and check tolerance across R-belt guide(~0.15mm difference)

1.    Checked end and middle of L-belt and R-belt height wrf to handler base plate.(~0.44mm)

2.    Perform hand height calibration on Input Arm, found out Hand A lower than the rest.

3.    Checked HOFS of Hand A 103, changed HOFS value to 550 and checked height of Hand A with other hands OK.


 


 

Cause

Service History

Subject: NS-7000 device floating and unable to pickup empty tray

Handler Model: NS-7000 (S/N: 181260)

Controller: RC520

Date: 10 May 2012


 

Symptom

NS-7000 device floating and unable to pickup empty tray.


 


 

Action

1.Customer reported that they had encounter high frequencies floating error at input shuttle.
-Input arm pick up hand adjusted with shuttle alignment jig.
-Performed Height calibration on input hands.
-Fine tuning for loader and input shuttle 1& 2 .

2. Found the empty tray hand unable to pickup the empty tray.
-Low vacuum reading found at the empty tray vacuum sensor unit -1.8PSI.(compared with another handler is -3.8PSI)
-Swapped the 60x100 vacuum pad but error still persist.
-Low vacuum reading solved after swapped the vacuum pump from other handler.
-Handler released to production.


 


 

Cause


 

Remarks

Sometimes, for some empty trays, the tray pocket for vacuum suction is not at centre of tray, so need to change "Pickup Up Position" (from usual 67.90mm) in [Tray Form], so that empty tray can be pickup.

So it may not be tray pad worn out or Empty tray arm position is out.

Subject: Monitor turn to blue screen

Handler Model: NS-8080(S/N: 181417, STATS ChipPac Ltd)

Controller: RC520

Date: 3 Apr 2008


 

Symptom

Monitor screen turn blue when collecting UPH data on 5x5 package for Octal and Quad sites.


 


 

Action

ON/OFF handler and everything turn fine but HMI unable to startup.

Reinstall HMI ver1.91 but problem still persist.

Uninstall HMI but still the same therefore uninstall SPEL and HMI follow by reinstall back the SPEL and HMI and everything back to normal.


 


 

Cause

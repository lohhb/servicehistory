Service History

Subject: NS-6040 new CPU Board installed but unable to detect Drive Unit

Handler Model: NS-6040 (NST-25)

Controller: SRC520

Date: 1 & 2 Nov 2011


 

Symptom

ASE(M) NS6040 NST-25 New CPU Board installed and unable to detect Drive Unit (DU).

 


 

Action

1) All heater unable to ON/OFF.
2) Swap CU and able to heat up.
3) Found COM connector wrongly connected. For NS-6000, Heater use COM1

 Returned to correct connection and able to ON/OFF.

4) Motor/Power status shows ??? in SPEL bottom-right screen
 5) Removed old LAN card as it will cause address conflict.
6) CPU Board Bios setting IRQ5 and IRQ10 to Legacy ISA.
7) Still unable to detect DU.
8) Replaced HDD from another running handler, same problem.
9) Replaced CU from another running handler, same problem.
10) Advised to check handler wiring connection from DU to CU.
11) Resit all DU to CU connection.
12) Able to detect DU now but Door Open on MMI and from SPEL shows E-Stop and Safety Door.
13) Replaced HDD and CU to confirm CU is ok but same problem.
14) Measured but no 24V on door sensor thus suspect door open error.
15) Measured SKP-387A in PSUNT, incoming P6 has +24V supply.
16) Out going found no 24V (TP30 Pin 5).

Can Tower light turn for Red output Bit 136, Yellow Output Bit 137, Green Output Bit 138, Buzzer Output Bit 139?
 17) To replace SKP-387A board to verify.


 


 

Cause

Resit all DU cables to CU (SRC520) solved unable to detect DU problem, it is due to loose connection of Motion cables.

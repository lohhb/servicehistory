Subject: NS-7000 Contact Counter kinds auto reset at every “Initial Start”

Handler Model: NS-7000 (Amkor, EPSON020, S/N: 181131)

Controller: RC520

Date: 16 May 2008


 

Symptom

MMI 2.0i1 reported by EPSON with software bug.


 


 

Action

Advised to upgrade MMI to 2.0k, ok.


 


 

Cause


 

Remarks

MMI 2.0i and 2.0j were reported to have software bug that caused Contact Counter kinds to auto reset at every Initial Start.

Refer 0087-NS7K.


 

MMI 2.0h was reported causing hang up at EEMS, refer 0083-NS7K.

Controller: RC520

Date: 27 Jan 2010


 

Symptom

Through the event log, "Z axis servo error was detected" (WAR01298) and "RS485 UnKnown error" (WAR01299) happened and the system has to reset and power off in order to reset the error.

 

I saw once during the production was that after the input arm pick up devices from loader and released to hotplate 2 but only 4 devices from Hand A-D have releases to hotplate 2 and devices from Hand E-H still attached on the hands and the whole system hang there.

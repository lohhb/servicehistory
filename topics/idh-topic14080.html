Service History

Subject: NS-8080 Socket Air (Jet Heater) temperature issue

Handler Model: NS-8000 series using Socket Air

Controller: RC520

Date: 17 Dec 2010


 

Symptom

NS-8080 Socket Air (Jet Heater) temperature issue.


 


 

Action

Received information from Hidaka. Settings on air heater alarm device should set at 200 DegC.

Found out both Temperature Warning Units TMP11 and TMP12 settings not same.

Found out solenoid valve on the air heater require to manual trigger if not air will not to

supply to Socket air heater(beside airflowmeter, trigger on control valve YV136). (Same condition as ASE)


 

TMP11 settings: 0 x100, 5 x10 and 0 x1   => 50 DegC

TMP12 settings: 2 x100, 0 x10 and 0 x1   => 200 DegC

Heat up socket air heater to 90 DegC, but socket air 1 only able to heat up to 68 DegC and socket air 2, OK.

Corrected Temperature Warning Unit TMP11 settings( Same as TMP12)

Heat up socket heater to 90DegC, OK.

Performed OMRON temperature auto-tuning at 130 DegC.

Completed and both socket air 1 and 2 temperature stable at 130 DegC)


 


 

Cause


 


 

Remarks

Refer email on 26 Apr 2013 on NS8080 Jet Heater (socket blower) installation procedure.

Refer to 0268-NS8K.

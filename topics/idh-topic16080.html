Service History

Subject: NS-8080 Auto stop with no alarm and chamber ionizer's cable broken

Handler Model: NS-8080 8 Hands High UPH (UTAC)

Controller: RC520

Date: 26 & 27 May 2011


 

Symptom

NS8080-11 handler auto stop with no alarm(25 May to 26 May)

- 25 May 1 time, 26 May 2 times reported by line.

- Did not encounter the issue during monitoring.

- Hidaka request for SPEL CT screen shot when problem happens.

- Informed to Ray and continue monitoring


 

NS8080-11 handler auto stop with no alarm
 - Handler pulled offline for dry cycle running check on 26 May evening.

- 27 May morning found handler stop running and in ‘reset’ condition with no alarm.

- Take screenshot of spel, collect event log, log folder, spel error history and setup file as per Hidaka san request.

- Heard arching sound from chamber and found ionizer cable damaged and shorting to the cable cover.

- Temporary use high temperature tape to cover up the exposed cable and continue dry cycle run. Informed to Ray. Yet to confirm if this is root cause for hang up issue.

- To request for a warranty claim.


 


 


 

Action

6 Jun 2011, Hidaka-san received the replacement ionizer of ND-503T-EP3 from Japan.


 

Follow up, refer 0338-NS8K.

Subject: NS-8040 sometime Output hand B unable to pickup device

Handler Model: NS-8040 (S/N:181207)

Controller: RC520

Date: 29~30 Sep 2009


 

Symptom

-In Hot mode, sometimes Output Hand B cannot pickup device at output shuttle 1 or shuttle 2 pocket B. Even sometimes Retry also cannot pickup. Replaced rubber cup for Hand B and also swapped pickup tip from Hand A, Auto Hand Height calibration performed also same problem.


 


 

Action

Check wiring at vacuum unit of output Hands, clean filter and swapped "Valve for counter air¡± (purge air ) of Hand A and Hand B, same problem.

Swapped "Valve for Supply Air" (vacuum unit) for Hand A & Hand B, pickup error of device migrated to Output Hand A.

Confirmed that Valve for Supply Air problem.


 

30 Sep 2009 Latching valve for ejector( Valve for Vauum unit)) replaced for Output Hand B.

Part Code: R35N700011000 (VQ110L-5LO-X21)


 

Refer warranty claim S0391


 

Cause

Faulty Latching valve for (vacuum) ejector.

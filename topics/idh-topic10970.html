Service History

Subject: NS-8040 Index Arm 2 Abnormal noise during contact to socket

Handler Model: NS-8040 (S/N:181207)

Controller: RC520

Date: 2 Oct 2009


 

Symptom

Received 2x2 adapter kit for NS-7000 c/k for M2900(LF2BGA189 8x8) for Quad site.

Convert handler to 2x2 setup, fine-tune output shuttle timing sensor.

-Found out arm2 during contact very loud and caused guide pins loosen on dockplate, need to verify with Index Arm Alignment Jig.


 


 

Action

- In SPEL CT, activate I/O Output Bit 245~247 to activate compliance unit.

- Index Arm 2 check with Index Arm Alignment jig, Arm 2 unable to fit into the alignment Jig.

- Loosen 4 bolts above Index Arm 2, alignment Arm 2 to fit into alignment jig and tighten bolts.

-Check Head Arm 2 Head 5/6 and Head 7/8 co-planarity gap for less than 20 micron with shim, ok.

- Reteach P51, P22 & P24.

- Check alignment on Index Arm 1 at P50, ok.

- 1 tray of M2900 device visual mechanical check with scanner after HOT mode run x05 times.

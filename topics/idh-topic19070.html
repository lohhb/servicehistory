Service History

Subject: NS-8080 During hot mode running at 100 deg, encountered chamber ionizer warning alarm. Found Kasuga ionizer EP3 alarm light sometimes blink

Handler Model: NS-8080 (S/N: 182284, H47-NS88)

Controller: RC520  HMI Ver 8G11.51-SAS

Date: 13 Mar 2013


 

Symptom

H47-NS88(182284)  during Amb/Hot jamrate.
-Issue: During hot mode running at 100 deg, encountered chamber ionizer warning alarm.

Found Kasuga ionizer EP3 alarm light sometimes blink.

No blinking when handler is at pause state or ambient mode.

Decay time measured (+)1.4sec, (-)1.5sec, Balance 0.4 volt which is normal.

Customer feedback many of their NS8080 having the same condition.

This error happed mainly in NS-8080 with 4 Input Hands(HMI VerP12.57-SAS)

.


 

Action

Feedback to Ogata san and sent video to EPSON.


 

15 Apr 2013 email by Ogata-san:

Regarding During Hot mode, blinking chamber Ionizer’s LED issue,

According to EPSON said, “This is under EPSON specification and normal.”

They roughly explain to me about mechanism or sequence of the error…But I need to double check with them something.

So I will be share with you it later on.


 

16 Apr 2013 email by Ogata-san:

Finally I received their comments.

I told you guys that it is EPSON specification and it is normal.

Basically Ionizer is controlling Ion balance and discharge.

Condition of the alarm is; (For Handler alarm)

1. To be over ion current.

2. To be ion balance unstable.


 

Specification of the above two items.

1. Ion current: 2uA±0.5uA for more than 100msec.

2. Ion balance: 0V±30V(Static voltage) for more than 100msec.

Condition of Alarm (Warning) is; (For ND503T)

It should be above specification situation for more than 20usec.

Hence ASE-S condition could be ion current or ion balance out of spec for 20usec.

During Hot mode, Corona discharge could be more appear then Ambient.

Then Corona discharge is disturbing (Break) an ion balance.

Hence ND503T is more blinking the Warning LED then ambient mode.

All specifications already set by Ionizer makers. We(included EPSON) are unable to change.

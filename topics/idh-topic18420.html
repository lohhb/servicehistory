Service History

Subject: NS-8080 High UPH 8 Hands Output Hand A no torque when power on

Handler Model: NS-8080 High UPH 8 Hands

Controller: RC520

Date: 15 May 2012


 

Symptom

15 May 2012: UTAC - NS8080-13 Output hand A no torque when Front Operation Panel Power On.


 


 

Action

- yesterday Output hand B no torque and stepper driver verified to be faulty.
- Requested spare to replace on Output hand B. But found hand A no torque also.
- Swapped stepper motor cable of hand A with hand B, problem migrated to hand B. Hand A stepper motor ok.
- Verified hand A stepper driver on hand B has torque, hand A stepper driver ok.
- Check continuity of X1 connector wirings on Output SKP456 board ok.
- Resit all connectors on Output SKP456 and swapped with Input SKP456, all hands have torque.
- Cycle run ok. Under monitoring.


 


 

Cause

Loose connection of connectors at SKP456.

Service History

Subject: SCS  NS8080 S/N: 181579 input shuttle device present fiber amplifier sensor shows negative values.

Handler Model: NS-8080 (S/N: 181579)

Controller: RC520

Date: 30~31 Mar 2010


 

Symptom

SCS  NS8080 Input shuttle device present sensor shows negative values.

Threshold setting showing (-1999 for shuttle 1 and -30 for shuttle 2).

Tired to adjust the threshold back to 600, at most could adjust was to -27.

Power tuning of the amplifier also done but failed to recover.


 


 

Action

Checking of amplifier setting and threshold values found threshold value at negative value but unable to adjust back to 600. Swapped with other amplifier and problem solved. Amplifier was removed from another handler to to service S/N: 181579 due to urgent need of handler.


 

Initialized the amplifier setting and set back to Epson setting. Sensor and amplifier working fine after initialized

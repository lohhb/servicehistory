Service History

Subject: NS-8040 Arm2 SiteA vacuum always on

Handler Model: NS-8040 (UTAC, NS80-16 S/N:181332)

Controller: RC520

Date: 16 Jul 2008


 

Symptom

NS-8040 reported by customer that Index Arm 2 SiteA vacuum always on.


 


 

Action

Dismantle Index vacuum valve manifold and found out vacuum valve for Index Arm 2 SiteA vacuum was manually turn on.

Turn off vacuum valve, O.K.


 

Found out Index Arm1 SiteC vacuum unit having abnormal display and unable to go into setting mode.

Replaced faulty vacuum switch unit by spare. O.K.

Raised Warranty for mentioned faulty item.


 


 

Cause

Faulty Index vacuum unit.

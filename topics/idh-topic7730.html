Subject: Issues for NS-6040 CPU Upgrading on NS6040 S/N: 12686

Handler Model: NS-6040 (StatsChippac)

Controller: SRC520

Date: 15 Oct 2008


 

Symptom

Issues during CPU Upgrading on NS6040 S/N: 12686:

a) SRC error occurred at Shuttle 1 during running check.

b) Open/close error happened during running check found no air supply to the variable hand.


 


 

Action

a) UPH conducted from 0sec to 6sec and backup the system before CPU upgrading.

850MHz CPU board was installed on the SRC controller.

System BIOS was configured for IRQ 5 & 10 as Legacy ISA.

Win98, MMI and SPEL were installed using CPU upgrading CD.

Upgrading of the MMI to ver C7.14-SSY and restoration of the backup files.

SRC error occurred at Shuttle 1 during running check.


 

b) Teaching of shuttle 1 carried out found P21 and P23 alignment out by 1mm and 6mm respectively. Open/close error happened during running check found no air supply to the variable hand. Adjusted the air speed controller.

UPH conducted from 0sec to 6sec after CPU upgrading.

Dummy run on 14x20 found Shuttle 2 alignment out when index arm pickup devices from shuttle. Teaching carried out on Shuttle 2. Alignment at  Auto 1-3 and Fix 1-3 out therefore have to re-align.

Service History

Subject: Device failed ESD on NS7080W S/N:121286, NS7KW-01 at Xilinx

Handler Model: NS--7080W (Xilinx, S/N: 121286)

Controller: RC520

Date: 3 Jul 2008


 

Symptom

Q240 was ran on these handler with high reject which failed ESD.


 


 

Action

First suspect is that handler ESD failed, thus the following checks were carried out.

Grounding check carried out on handler all areas (loader, unloaders, reloaders, automats, input shutters, output shutters, input arm's hands, output arm's hands and index arms) found good , that is, less than 5 ohm.

ESD decay time check carried out found all ionizer fans less than 5 sec.

All the above checks carried out and found within specifications.

With the above good result, Alan said that the reject may be due to assembly of device.

Had also checked with Alan that other devices were also ran on these handler with no ESD failure.

The above checks were initiated by Xilinx management.

Service History

Subject: R01 / TR-60 / S/N : 121175

1. Device dropped after test during running RT.

2. Handler trip during Change to Hot Mode.

Handler Model: TR-60 (S/N: 121175)

Controller: RC520

Date: 25~27 Oct 2010


 

Symptom

R01 / TR-60 / S/N : 121175

1. Device dropped after test during running ambient.

2. Handler trip during change from Ambient Mode to Hot Mode.


 


 

Action

1. Encounter Output Hand B Pick up error on Output Shuttle pocket D, device missing.

- Checking purge air for Index Arm 1 & 2, found 1-B, 1-C, 2-A, 2-B and 2-D strong purge air.

- Adjust Index Arm 1 & 2, purge air 1 & 2, found 1-B, 1-C, 2-A, 2-B and 2-D.

- Found Nest worn out for Index 1-A & B.

- Replace Index 1-A & B Nest.

- Encounter device pick up error on Soak Boat for Index Arm 2-A, Filter cleaning.

- Encounter device missing on Output Shuttle pocket D, change release height to Output Shuttle to -27.5 mm, production monitoring.  


 


 

2. Found out sleeving for Thermo fuse x02 cut by Thermo Fuse's metal holders, suspect this cause shorting.

- Heat up Handler to 128DegC without Thermo Fuse's holder, ok.

- Wrap exposed part of thermo fuses with sleeving and secured with Thermo fuse's metal holders.

- Heat up Handler to 128DegC without Thermo Fuse's holder, ok.


 


 

Cause

1) Index Arm device after test missing on shuttle due to purge air too strong and release height issue.

2) Thermo fuse body short to handler body (as sleeving broken) caused TR-60 to power trip when switch to hot temp.


 

Remarks

The speed of Tray elevator for Up/Down cannot be adjusted according email by Hidaka on 28 Oct 2010.


 

Pickup error, check:

- pickup height

- check is there any vacuum when vacuum activated

- broken/loose/bend tubing

- air fitting/gasket is it tighten

- check vacuum strong or not when vacuum activated

- vacuum sensitivity

- plunger

- o-ring worn out on plunger

- suction cup worn out

Service History

Subject: NS8080-01 8 hands handler having "Hand up sensor error" with Output Arm

Handler Model: NS-8080 (NS8080-01) 8 Hands High UPH

Controller: RC520

Date: 9 Dec 2010


 

Symptom

UTAC  NS8080-01 8 hands handler having "Hand up sensor error" with Output Arm.


 


 

Action

Frequently seen output arm pause at output shuttle 1 and hand up sensor error happened.

Check all actuators' ball splines.

Found Hand D, F and G rough movement with power off.

Oil up ball spline, and check movement gets better.

Perform hand height calibration.

Dry cycle ok.

Release to customer and advise customer to monitor.

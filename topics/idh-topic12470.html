Service History

Subject: NS-8080 chamber heater trip during production

Handler Model: NS-8080 (S/N: 181634, 03 ) STATSChippac

Controller: RC520 (S/N: 03-02093)

Date: 26 Apr 2010


 

Symptom

Reported by customer that chamber heater's breaker trip during production.


 


 

Action

-Check chamber heater AC connector, not short to ground.

-Check incoming AC power, not short to ground.

-Removed the chamber heater case and pull out the chamber heater, try to loosen heater wire clamp screws to check whether wire got short to ground. found ok.

-Check on chamber fuse insulation found in good condition.

-Disconnect the wire connected on the chamber temperature fuse and check the DC found ok.

-Try to heat up temperature to 50 degree and couldn't heat up to 130 degree found the temperature MAX Range was accidentally change to 90 degree.

-Change back the MAX range to 130 degree.

(C:\NS8000\Config\Range\temperature.def  =>under [[Mode]], set " Temperature=50,130,30"

-Dry run with 100% speed and 130 degree for 5 hrs handler ok.

-Release handler to production.

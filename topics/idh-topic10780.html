Subject:IDT(M) Using NS-8040 Shuttle Board on NS-7000 requires rewiring in NS-7000.

Handler Model: NS-7000

Controller: RC520

Date: 20 Oct 2009


 

Symptom

Using NS-8040 Shuttle Board SKP 412 -2 on NS-7000 requirement rewiring in NS-7000.


 


 

Action

15 pins connector

It has Shuttle 1 and 2.

You can separate 5 pins each.

But connect to 412-2 are as follows;


 

1 to 5 pin connect to SLT

6 to 10 pin connect to D1

11 to 15 pin connect to D2


 

Note: Please check Shuttle 1 and 2.


 

DIO connector :

412-Nf is 8 pins connector

412-2 is 9 pins connector

But, wiring quantity is same.

So, if you have 412 NF connector, please use it to 412-2.


 

Wiring conversion from SKP412NF to SKP412-2. Pls refer to email below.

 

Also, additional info for SKP412-2 5pin assignment:

 

SKP 412-2 Pin assignment 


 

Emitter

Pin 1

24v

Brown

Pin 2

signal

Black

Pin 3

0v

Blue

Receiver

Pin 4

24v

Brown

Pin 5

0v

Blue


 

It is advisable to use multimeter to check for 24V on Pin 1, Pin 3 and Pin 4, Pin 5 on the board first before connecting the new sensor molex connectors on it.(Fix the board with the CN4 and CN3 connectors first, power ON 24V, measure Pin voltage to confirm 24V on the correct pins.)

 

For CN4 connector on the SKP412-2 board, connector is different from SKP412NF. So you need to get a 9 pin molex connector and transfer the wiring from the 8 pin molex connector. Wiring sequence is same, just ignore the additional empty pin on the 9 pin connector.


 

Wiring conversion from Shuttle Board SKP412NF to SKP412-2 for 5pin assignment on each connector(SLT, D1 and D2).

Cut & trim DIO connect CN4 so that the 8 pin connector can plug directly into the 10 pin connector on Shuttle board SKP 412 -2, leaving to pin exposed.

Replaced to Shuttle board SKP 412 -2.

3) H14-NS88 s/n: 181594

Epson is quite sure that this handler was shipped out to ASE with old design ball spline shaft.

However, we were also informed that ASE found new design of ball spline shaft on this handler too.

Although Epson is not able to check their shipping record if they shipped out the old or new design replacement

ball spline shafts over 3 times, Epson is pretty sure that they 3rd time shipment is the new design ball spline shaft.

Hence, this system now is installed with new design ball spline shaft and should not expect failures with it.

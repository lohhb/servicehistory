Service History

Subject: SCS NS-8040 Unable to dock up docking plate

Handler Model: NS-8040

Controller: RC520

Date: 9 Dec 2011


 

Symptom

SCS NS-8040 unable to dock up docking plate.


 


 

Action

- Found out the docking plate guide pins wrong dimension. Guide pins changed.

Unable to pick up devices on shuttles and index arms vacuum low.
- due to index arm alignment out and shuttles guide pins dimension issues.
- Shuttles guide pins changed and able to pick up from shuttles.
- Auto and manual teaching on index arm alignment carried out but the alignment still out. Temporary release for online temperature collection.
- Temperature reads slightly low during the online temperature collection therefore, temperature adjusted on the index arms and socket.

Subject: EEMS NS-8080 Power trip when pressed START or HOME

Handler Model: NS-8080

Controller: RC520

Date: 9 Oct 2009


 

Symptom

NS-8080 power trip when pressed START or HOME.


 


 

Action

- Found 400W(Z Axis) servo driver (R13B070114; CACR-04-3U3C-E)

in DU1 burnt mark on PCB(under the capacitor).

- AC/DC board (SKP 447A-1) 2 pcs of rectifiers(covered by rectangular heatsink); customer compared resistance measurement between to rectifiers, one rectifier with resistance less than 10ohms, the other rectifier with about 270ohms.

- waiting for customer to buy spare parts for replacement.

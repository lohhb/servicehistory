Error 5004   Initialization   time-out   error   with  Absolute Encoder.


 

The  Absolute  Encoder  data  has  not  been  received when SPEL CT was launched.

Check on the hardware settings.

Check if the Incremental Encoder is connected.
Replace the motor in case of the encoder failure.
Replace the Drive Unit in case of DMB failure.

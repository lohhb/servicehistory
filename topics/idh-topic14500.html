Subject: NS-8000 Configuration And Range

Handler Model: NS-8000 series

Controller: RC520


 


 

Handler configuration "Config" such as Input Hand pitch, Lane Configuration, Chamber Door Exist Sensor, Loader Shift 60mm, enable rotator, shuttle on ionizer:

C:\NS8000\Config\Handler.def 


 

For Setup Test site X-pitch and Y-pitch minimum and maximum Range:

C:\NS8000\Config\Range\HandlerCondition.rng


 

Disable Socket Heater (Display) in HMI for NS-8080/NS-8160:

Click [Temp.Offset], uncheck "Socket Heater"


 

Disable Socket Air Blow (Display) in HMI for NS-8000:

Click [Temp.Offset], uncheck "Socket Air Blow"

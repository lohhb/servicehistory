Service History

Subject: Different in Contact Force

Handler Model: NX1032XS (S/N: 182347)

Controller: RC520 (S/N: 00504), DUBOX030 (S/N: 100004), PSUNT353 (P892)

Date: 25 Oct 2012


 

Symptom

FF900 octal loadcell verification check, found different force value between at Index arm 1 and Index arm 2.

 


 

Action

Found the Index arm 2 Socket Layout Kit (SLK) O-ring missing for Compliance Unit.


 


 

Cause

Missing O-ring for SLK's caused air leak.

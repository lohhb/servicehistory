Service History

Subject: NS-7080 Index Arm 1 Head A and G no vacuum.

Handler Model: NS-7080(STATSChippac)

Controller: RC520

Date: 3 Jul 2008


 

Symptom

There was vacuum from the main manifold till the Index arm 1 manifold.

 


 

Action

Further checking found Head A and G tubing broken at the unions.

Tubing re-fitted.

Vacuum check found with no abnormality.


 


 

Cause


 


 

Remarks

Broken tubing due to design issue

Refer 0155-NS7K on the new improve design.

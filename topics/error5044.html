Error 5044   Speed  error  overflow  in  the High-power state.


 

The  speed  error  (an  irregular  speed)  is  detected  in  the High-power state.

Check on the settings for WEIGHT for the load values.

Check  the  Manipulator

(1)  if  the  arm  moves  around smoothly when  it  is  moved  around  by hands,  (2)  if the arm's  movement  is  tight  and  secure  when  it  is  moved around by hands, 

(3) if there is any hitches with the arm when it is moved around by hands, 

(4) if the belt is not slackened when checked disassembled and 

(5) if the arm does not hit the brake when it is moved around while the electromagnetic brake is turned off.

Check if the Manipulator's arm is not colliding with the peripheral units around.

Check on the hardware settings in SPEL CT.

Check on the power cable connection.

Check on the power cables inside the Manipulator for any loose pins, cable disconnection or short-circuit.

Check on the main power voltage  (INPUT voltage at the Drive Unit) and make sure that it is within the range.

Replace the motor driver.

Replace the Drive Unit.

Replace the motor in case of motor/encoder failure.

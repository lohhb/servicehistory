Service History

Subject: NS-7080W Output Arm unable to complete Auto teaching on Automat

Handler Model: NS-7080W (NS7KW-08)

Controller: RC520

Date: 2, 4 May 2012


 

Symptom

02 May 2012: Xilinx – Output arm unable to complete teaching on automat tray(NS7KW-08, MMI M8.10-SXC).


 


 


 

Action

2 May 2012

Software M8.11-SXC ready, but some of their handlers got higher version M11.13-SXC.
Alex decided to standardize software based on latest. Feedback to Hidaka san and he sent M12.14-SXC based on M11.13-SXC.


 

4 May 2012

Installed MMI v.M12.14-SXC.
Checked/verified output arm autoteaching at automat position, ok. Automat tray jig able to return to automat and complete teaching cycle.
Release MMI M12.14-SXC disc to Alex.
Dry cycle handler ok.


 


 

Cause

Bug in MM1 M8.10-SXC.

MMI M12.14-SXC solved bug on output arm autoteaching at automat position.

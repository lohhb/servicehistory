Service History

Subject: DU1 Axis 2 Motor Driver Module and AC/DC Board faulty

Handler Model: NS-8040 (S/N: 181863)

Controller: RC520

Date: 28 Oct 2007


 

Symptom

Halfway running production and whole handler tripped.

Handler trip upon HOME/START.

Motor ON Drive Unit 1(DU1) in SPEL CT, handler trip.

Motor ON Drive Unit 2, (DU)ok.


 


 

Action

Found DU1 Axis 2 Empty Tray Arm 100W Motor Driver Module (servo driver) badly burnt.

But after swapping with new Motor Driver Module also cause power trip.

Swapped AC/DC Board(Motor Power Unit) in Drive Unit Box, ok.

  

 

100W Motor Driver Module (R13B070112)

AC/DC Board (SKP447A-1)


 


 

Cause

Power trip was caused by faulty 100W Motor Driver Module and AC/DC Board in Drive Unit.


 


 

Remarks

Warranty claim:

S0261 - 100W Motor Driver Module

S0262 - AC/DC Board


 

When press START or Home, the Drive Units will activate SSR to turn AC servo motor on.

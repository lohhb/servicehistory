Service History

Subject: NS-7080W After conversion to FG484 23x23 Octal encountered:

1)Automat Elevator abnormal noise

2)Index Arm2 stuck in socket

Handler Model: Xilinx NS-7080W(S/N: 121080; NS7KW-03) MFG Jun 2006

Controller: RC520

Date: 10 Aug 2010


 

Symptom

After customer conversion to FG484 23x23 Octal encountered:

1)Automat Elevator abnormal noise

2)Index Arm2 stuck in socket


 


 

Action

-Automat slot 2, 3 & 9 bent, tray clamper near to elevator slightly bent

-knock back slot 2,3 & 9 and tray clamper for flatness

-Reteach robot 10 tranverser P1(inside Automnat) from Y= 4.5mm to 1mm

-Adjust sensor input sensor SQ212(AM_TRY_STU; Tray slot in Elevator detection)

-Adjust robot 10 Tranverser HOME sensor's slit plate as Y position changed after reteach P1


 

-24Vdc in PSU check for supply to ICOS, ok

-Customer verify that ICOS motor cannot move when power off and check with ICOS vendor and suspect motor for ICOS spoilt


 

-Index Arm 1 & 2 for P50 & P51 check, ok

-Layout kit for Arm 2 loose when latched, customer just changed the metal latches

-Converted to FG456 23x23 Octal, Arm 2 layout kit air leak.

-Vibration can be seen on the layout kit.

-Customer will add shimm to the metal latches, but mean time just disable Index Arm 2


 

Cause

1) Automat crashed

2) Layout kit problem due to metal latch

3) Icos motor spoilt.


 

Remarks

Index Arm 2 found SLK "vibrating". Suggesting the latch/clip tension NG due to previous history

of SLK stuck.

SLK stuck after conditional buyoff found 3rd party nest/mix kit used although mix kit is compatible from device point of view but actual change kit like guide pin may be different.

Mix kit found some nest to be tight with guide base guide pin while others ok. This is primary reason for SLK stuck after testing because some nest very tight fit to guide pin.

Guide pin wrong size or Guide pin become loose on socket guide jig also can cause Index Arm stuck in sock.

Coupled with weak latch/clip, SLK dislodged with index arm moving up.

Correct meta Latch of parts No is TL-29A, this is supplied by Tochigiya.

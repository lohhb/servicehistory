Service History

Subject:  NS-8040 Index Head 1/2 display 520 deg on HMI during heat up.

Handler Model: NS-8040 (S/N: 181871, NS80-39) UTAC

Controller: RC520 (S/N: 03-10150)

Date: 9 Feb 2012


 

Symptom

NS-8040 (NS80-39) Index Head 1/2 display 520 deg on HMI during heat up.


 


 

Action

- Check connection and resit connector still same.

- Consulted and discussed with Roel, temporary remove temperature sensor from NS80-37 ATC handler since temperature feedback dependent on ATC Thermal Head.

- Removed Head 5/6 temperature sensor from NS80-37 ATC and replaced in NS80-39.

- Heat up handler OK.

- Raise warranty replacement for NS80-39.


 


 

Cause

Faulty PT sensor for Index 1 Head 1/2.

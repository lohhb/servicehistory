Error 5016   Absolute Encoder backup alarm.


 

The encoder detected that all the three power sources (5V, the battery power and the installed capacitor) are down.


 

Check on the battery voltage(3.6V typically).


 

Reset the encoder.


 

Replace the motor in case of the encoder failure.


 

Note for NS-8000 series handlers, if Error 5016 Absolute Encoder backup alarm occurred and all motors indicates ?? in SPEL CT, perform either step 1 or 2 of the following:

1) Install SPEL CT 3.11ds

2) Use Recovery CD for SEIKO- EPSON NS8000 FD07S109, Ver 1.56 (12/11/2007) or later version.

Then perform the normal restore of SPEL Project & System, HMI restoring data(RUN folder, CONFIG folder and setup files) or using BackupSan8 to restore.

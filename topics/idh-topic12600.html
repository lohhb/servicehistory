Service History

Subject: NS-8080W encountered device pickup error at Index Arm

Handler Model: NS-8080W(S/N: 181464)

Controller: RC520

Date: 5 May 2010


 

Symptom

CP132 8x8x1.0mm Quad(Test-site X-pitch 75mm, Y-pitch 80mm) as FG456 kit removed for production use

-pick and place alignment check, encountered device pick up error but device already on Index Arm 2-B.


 


 

Action

 -Found vacuum reading -37kpa and plunger cannot retract.

 Check vacuum sensitivity set at -45kpa.

-check all contactors for Index Arm 1& 2.

-Replaced small o-ring for plunger 1-B, 2-A, 2-B & 2-D.

-pick and place alignment for Ambient & Hot

-Ambient jamrate 1/5000

-Hot jamrate 1/5000


 


 

Cause

O-ring on plunger worn out cause vacuum leakage.

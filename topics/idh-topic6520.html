Service History

Subject: NS-7080 (NS7k-06) device drop Index arm 1 socket B

Handler Model: NS-7080 (Xilinx)

Controller: RC520

Date: 2 Apr 2008


 

Symptom

Using Drop and direct contact, device drop at Index Arm 1 socket B.


 

Action

Vacuum unit cleaning.

Check index arm tubing, OK.

Check incoming and outgoing vacuum for all sites. OK

Pick up from shuttle OK.

Move to test site OK.

After test, moving out of test site, device drop.

Vacuum check of index arm at lowest and highest point. OK

Verify contact height, original over plunge by 0.6-0.7 mm. Suspect due to workpress assembly(contactor) design of XAP, while over plunge and sudden movement up, compliant erect causes vacuum leak/push device lead to device drop. Sensor “OFF” only after test when move up.

New contact height, running check with Drop mode and Direct contact mode, OK.

Released to production. Will monitor overnight for problem

Total 2.5hrs


 

Cause

Suspect due to workpress assembly(contactor) design of XAP, while over plunge and sudden movement up, compliant erect causes vacuum leak/push device lead to device drop. Sensor “OFF” only after test when move up.


 


 

Remarks

There was a case of device drop for both Index Arm 1 & 2, after replacement of Remote I/O Board, problem solved.


 

Check points for Index Arm Device drop:

- vacuum cup condition, device drop more frequently in hot mode?

- o-ring worn out on plunger

-Check diameter/tolerance of guide pins on socket guide plate

-Check any broken/bent tubing or tubing pressed by cable tie on SLK

-Check air fitting/elbow or gasket not loose on SLK

-Check if guide pins bent/worn out

-Check vacuum filter dirty

-Check vacuum filter put properly

-Check seal for vacuum put properly and not torn

-Check plastic casing for vacuum filter not broken

-Check nest guide pin's hole

-Check o-ring on SLK

-Check metal hooks of Socket Layout Kit (SLK)

-Check correct brand and specification of latch use for (Index) Test Hands

-Check metal latch worn out at Index Test Hand

-Check Index teaching position P50 and P51

-Check if Index Arm crash before or Index Arm bent(Index Arm alignment check)

- if device never drop, adjust vacuum switch sensitivity


 

Correct metal latch of parts No is TL-29A, this is supplied by Tochigiya.

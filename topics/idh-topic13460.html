Service History

Subject: Intel (M) NS-7000 chamber connector burnt

Handler Model: NS-7000

Controller: RC520

Date: 4 Nov 2010


 

Symptom

Intel (M) NS-7000 chamber connector burnt.

Customer wired and soldered themselves pin to pin.

Upon heater turn on, chamber heater unable to cut off after reach set point. Up to 200+ degrees still unable to cut off.

Wiring confirm by customer as correct. Heater and PT sensor is OK.


 


 

Action

Advise customer to trace and swap SSR (Solid State Relay).

After swap with shuttle SSR, shuttle unable to cut off after reach set point.


 


 

Cause

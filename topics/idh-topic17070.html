Service History

Subject: INTEL(M) NS-8080 Device drop after testing

Handler Model: NS-8080

Controller: RC520

Date: 13 Oct 2011


 

Symptom

INTEL(M) NS-8080 Device drop after testing.


 


 

Action

1) Contactor swap but problem does not migrate.
2) Found contactor plunger stiff on affected contactor.
3) Pick up from shuttle strong vacuum.
4) During testing, found vacuum drop and device dropped into socket.
5) Customer using socket guide plate not made by KA.
6) Saw affected contactor slide into socket by +ve y.
7) KA socket guide plate also same problem.
8) Customer insisted on using socket guide plate not made by KA so rotate 180degrees to try again.
9) This time no vacuum drop and no device drop.
10) Suspect alignment/dimensioning issue.

Handler is OK and change kit related.

 


 

Cause

Change kit related issue.

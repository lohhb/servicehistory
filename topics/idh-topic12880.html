Service History

Subject: SEP NS-6040 Hard Disk Drive crashed

Handler Model: NS-6040 (S/N: 15738 #800, NS-6040-15, Mfg Sep 2002)

Controller: SRC520

Date: 11 & 14 Mar 2011


 

Symptom

NS-6040 HDD crashed.


 


 

Action

11 Mar 2011

1)   Loan spare HDD and SEP attempted manual recovery.

2)   Upon restart, unable to detect HDD.

3)   But HDD can be detected in another handler.

4)   Found HDD cradle pin bent towards each other causing short condition.

5)   Repaired cradle pin connector and able to detect HDD.

6)   Win95 installed, MMI and SPEL installed.

7)   But unable to launch SPEL.

8)   To engage service next week.


 

14 Mar 2011

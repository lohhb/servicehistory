Service History

Subject: NS-7000 PG Board error, encoder backup alarm, uniwire disconnection

Handler Model:

Controller:

Date: 11~12 Jan 2011


 

Symptom

Smart Modular customer NS70-01 encounter PG board error.

Replaced PG board from NS70-02 same problem.

NS70-01 SPEL failed to initialise and has fatal error.

Advise chuah to confirm NS70-02 first.


 


 

Action

NS70-02 encoder backup alarm and uniwire disconnection error.

Found wire of RI/O(red) broken. After repair, no more uniwire error.

Reset encoder and encounter battery alarm. Advise to check battery voltage.

0V and replaced with NS70-01 battery. Restart SPEL. OK.

All robots does not show ??? in SPEL taskbar. Able to motor on.

Calibrate and point teaching for all robots.

NS70-01, customer to purchase battery and continue troubleshooting.


 

12 Jan 2011

NS70-01 battery purchased. Encountered error 4103 motion control module initialization error.

NS70-01 PG board into NS70-02. No problem. Thus no PG board problem as informed by customer earlier.

Checked MIB 1,2,3 connection. Ok

Checked MIB 1,2,3 position and condition in CU. Ok

Checked MIB 1,2,3 jumper settings. Ok

SPEL failure to initialized means unable to detect DU.

Compare NS70-01 MIB with NS70-02 MIB. Same.

NS70-02 MIB into NS70-01, SPEL able to initialize.


 


 

Cause

NS70-01 PG board error does not appear after swapped PG board from NS70-02.


 

NS70-02 encoder backup alarm due to low voltage of lithium battery in DU.

NS70-02 Wire of RI/O (red) broken caused uniwire error.


 

4103 motion control module initialization error will appear when motor status is ???

Service History

Subject: NS-8040 During last week index arm reinforcement,NS-001 and NS-004 index arm 2 is found to have bad coplanarity issue.

Handler Model: NS-8040 (NS-001 & NS-004)

Controller: RC520

Date: 9 Dec 2010


 

Symptom

During last week index arm reinforcement, 001 and 004 index arm 2 is found to have bad coplanarity issue.


 


 

Action

For NS-001, 0.05mm shim able to slide between index head and alignment jig.

Adjust 4 screws + loctite 272.

Now 0.03mm unable to slide between index head and alignment jig.


 

For NS-004, found gap of more than 1mm.

Removed four screws to check index head assembly flatness with alignment jig. OK and 0.03mm shim unable to slide. Suspect screw height.

Attach index head assembly to compliant unit without screws. OK and 0.03mm shim unable to slide.

Tighten 1 round for each screws to compliant unit head.

OK and 0.03mm shim unable to slide and not more 1mm gap.

Both handlers released for production and request IFX to enable all sites for running to see of any yield issue.


 

Asami-san's report on his action at Infineon on Dec/9 as follows.


 

1) S/N 181452 (NS-001)

 - Using JIG and found about 0.5mm gap on part of Index Arm Head/#7 & #8

 - Adjusted screw force and made it back to correct condition.

 - did dummy run and understood the problem was taken care of.


 

2) S/N 181671 (NS-004)

 - Using JIG and found about 1.0mm gap on part of Index Arm Head/#7 & #8

 - Once took out 4 screws, adjusted and tightened them back with locktite.

 - did dummy run and understand the problem was taken care of.


 

According to Asami-san, condition for both handler should be OK now, but it would be better to monitor the performance for the time being.

For your reference.

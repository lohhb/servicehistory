Service History

Subject: UTAC- NS70-71 and NS70-72 input and output hands high resistance

Handler Model: NS-7000(181172, 181183)

Controller: RC520

Date: 15 Aug 2009


 

Symptom

UTAC - NS70-71 and NS70-72 input and output hands high resistance.


 


 

Action

NS70-71

-This handler ionizer upgraded was done by UTAC themselves. Found 24vdc tapped from handler 24vdc supply in PSUNT for all 5x KD-750B.

-Request for a spare power adaptor to be used on the 2 input and output loader side ionizer fans and measured resistivity on input/output hands to be good.


 

NS70-72

-This handler was upgraded by Getech. Found resistivity high for input/output hands when the input, output shuttle and fix tray fans turned on.

-Checked on the power tap and found resistivity high
 (80 ohms) on the grounding pins with reference to the handler's body.

Relocate the 2x power adaptors for the input,output shuttle and fix tray fans to the 100Vac supply on the PSUNT and measured the resistivity on the input/output hands to be good.

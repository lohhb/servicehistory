Service History

Subject: UTAC NS8080-05 output arm Y-axis jammed

Handler Model: NS-8080 (NS8080-05, S/N: 181725) 8 Hands High UPH

Controller: RC520

Date: 10 Nov 2010


 

Symptom

Output arm Y-axis jammed at the Mcal position. Unable to move.


 


 

Action

Removed output Y motor unit and loosen from belt, motor able to move.

Loosen output Y pulley unit, pulley able to move.

After Y-axis belt tension loosen, arm able to move again.

Cannot find anything stuck along the Y axis.

Re-Calibrate the Y-axis but during homing, encounter torque error for index arm and input arm(Z-phase sensor position of motor to mechanical sensor data different).

Perform MCORG for all robots, then able to complete Home.

Release to production and monitor 5 trays ok.


 


 

Cause

Subject: NS-8040 ATC water on socket caused Bin 7 and Temperature Controller A3h1 error

Handler Model: NS-8040

Controller: RC520

Date: 26 Nov 2010


 

Symptom

Information provided by Roel:

1) Water found on socket and causing Bin 7.

2) Temperature controller having A3h1 error, set temp 110DegC but thermal head temperature displayed 17DegC and causing condensation.

 


 

Action

Troubleshoot:

1) Intermittently thermal head unable to heat up to 110DegC, once reached more than 80 to 90DegC, controller will have A3h1 error and thermal head temperature will drop back to chiller temperature(19DegC).

2) Swapped thermal to another controller, also same.

3) Borrow thermal head from NS-6040 ATC, thermal able to heat up to 110DegC without having any A3h1 error.

4) Measured NS-8040 TC resistance on connector, found out to be 39Ω.

5) Measured NS-6040 TC resistance on connector less than .

6) Replaced TC on thermal head, measured resisitance less than 6Ω and heat up to 110DegC without A3h1 error.

7) Informed Roel to monitor situation as root-cause still unknown. It could be other parts on the thermal head which is defective.

 

Another issue is when thermal controller with A3h1 error thermal head temperature drop to less than 110°C, handler can run without any error.

The TC spare i used to replaced on NS-8040 ATC was from the spare UTAC received during NS-6040 ATC installation.

 

Action to be taken:

1) Please check current software v2.6 whether able to detect controller error and will the modified HMI software will be able to get feedback from thermal controller when having error.

2) Please check specification on TC for both NS-6040 and NS-8040, and prepare spare TC, thermistor and Peltier for thermal head as we still do not know which are the items are defective.

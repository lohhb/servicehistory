Service History

Subject: NS8040 Index arm alignment out

Handler Model: NS-8040 (S/N: 151205)

Controller: RC520

Date: 12 Dec 2011


 

Symptom

SCS NS8040 S/N:151205 Index arm alignment out.


 


 

Action

Adjusted the index arm 1 and 2 using alignment jig.

Contact height calibration found out the index arm alignment still out at the shuttle position. Therefore, index arm alignment carried out once again with one of the index arm plunged down to the shuttle.

Dummy running carried out found Index arm 1 Site 1 having device drop issue after test but the device was attached on the contactor. <=was vacuum sensitivity adjusted to correct threshold at the vacuum switch unit?

Changed contactor, did a contact height calibration and slowly lower the contact value but problem still persist.

Try adjusting the vacuum volume higher at vacuum valve and issue solved.

Handler release for production.

 


 

Cause


 


 

Remarks

If slow response of vacuum switch unit to detect device pickup, increase the vacuum volume by increasing the vacuum volume at vacuum unit.

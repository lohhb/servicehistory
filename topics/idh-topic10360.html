Service History

Subject: SRC error at Index Arm and Index Arm 2 hit shuttle guide pin

Handler Model: NS-6040 (S/N: 15477)

Controller: RC520

Date: 16 Jun 2009


 

Symptom

During buyoff encountered the following errors:

- During contact height calibration, SRC error occurred at index arm found that the homing position out.

- Index Arm 2 hit the shuttle guide pin when picking up devices. out.


 

Action

- During contact height calibration, SRC error occurred at index arm found that the homing position out.

Mcorg robot 1 for Index and mcal carried out to solve the issue.


 

- Index Arm 2 hit the shuttle guide pin when picking up devices.

Found out the shuttle alignment out.


 

17 Jun 2009

SCR error happened again during contact height cal. mcorg and Teaching on index arm carried out but problem still persist.

Restoring of pose data from previous backup solved the problem.


 

QFN 5x5 4sites alignment carried out with missing devices at both shuttles. After adjusting of counter air, counter air time and release height, number of missing devices reduce.

During alignment check found that the all 1x4 (4sites) floating sensors did not turn on and only 1x2 (2sites) floating sensors turn on. But these does gives any error during the alignment check.


 

24 Jun 2009

Troubleshooting of the several floating sensors does not turn on found that one of the wire at connector I/O2 partially broken. Wire re-work.

SRC error and some SPEL errors occurred again during index arm height calibration. Suspecting Z motor faulty. However, replaced Z motor but same error. So motor is ok.


 

29~30 Jun 2009 SRC error occurred during index arm height calibration

During installation of original servo pack, CT gain and offset was recorded.  Upon boot up, it was found out that all servo pack in DU 1's CT gain and offset were totally different from physical. Thus, physical figure keyed in and height calibration was carried out without problem.


 


 

Cause

SRC error occurred during index arm height calibration

-could be due to Index Arm homing position out.

-could be due to Servo pack (servo driver) CT Gain and CT Offset for DU1 having wrong value.


 

Index Arm 2 hit the shuttle guide pin when picking up devices.

Found out the shuttle alignment out.


 

Floating sensors not turn on due to wire connector IO 2 partially broken.

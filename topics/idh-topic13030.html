Subject: NS-8040 During production, the compliance unit cylinders of Arm 1 & 2 after test when moving up, the contactors seem moving in Y(minus and plus) direction

Handler Model: NS-8040 (NS-001)

Controller: RC520

Date: 22 Jul 2010


 

Symptom

Customer informed that during production the compliance unit cylinders of Arm 1 & 2 after test

when moving up, the contactors seem moving in Y(minus and plus) direction.


 


 

Action

Offline dry run handler(without docking), only with changekit. Run at 100% & 10% speed unable to see and abnormal movement at test area,  and no abnormal sound.

checked the bolts around test area where the socket guide plate is to be secured, found some bolts near G-10 material were loose, tighten the bolts.


 


 

Cause

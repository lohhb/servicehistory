Service History

Subject: NS-8080 8 Hands handler (S/N: 181655) hands replacement by Mikuni exclude Fixed Hands

Handler Model: NS-8080 High UPH 8 Hand (S/N: 181655, H15-NS88)

Controller: RC520 (S/N: 03-02115)

Date: 5~6 Sep 2011


 

Symptom

ASE – NS-8080 8 Hands handler (S/N: 181655) hands replacement by Mikuni exclude Fixed Hands.


 


 

Action

- Input & Output Hand Actuators replacement for moving Hands exclude fixed Handshaking
-use back Hand adaptors(do not loss the 0-ring inside Hand Adaptor)
-When Hands closed, the spacing between Hand A & C, C & E, E &G should be visual close about 0.2mm gap.
-Ensure each hand is perpendicular to shuttle surface(using L shape jig for checking)
-House keeping on wires & air tubings
-Close HMI, in Z Teach,
Individual HOME the Hand
Activate vacuum and check vacuum detection
Activate purge air and check purging
-Replaced to red metal pickup tip before Master Calibration
-Trigger vacuum for all input Hands and check vacuum detection level against the rib(to ensure no leakage before Master Calibration)
-Perform Master Calibration with jig using AutoTeach
-Output Hands also perform vacuum, purge air and Master Calibration
-Home Handler
-Robot 6, Shuttle 2 move to P22
-Input Hands pitch alignment using Hand pitch alignment jig on Input Shuttle 2
-Home Handler
-Robot 6, Shuttle 2 move to P24
-Output Hands pitch alignment using Hand pitch alignment jig on Output Shuttle 2
-AutoTeach (3 points) for Input & Output areas
-input & Output Hand Auto Hand height Cal at rib
-Dry run without device at 10% speed, found Input pick & place out by -2mm in X direction
-For Robot 2 pos data, all add +2mm for all X direction
-Install Changekit & Auto Contact height
-Run with device at 10% & 80% speed
-Pick & place alignment to Auto1
-Pick & place alignment for Auto1 to Fix 3

Service History

Subject: NS-7080W Device pick up error on index arm 1. Arm 1 disabled for production

Handler Model: NS-7080W (NS7KW-08)

Controller: RC520

Date: 26 Nov 2010


 

Symptom

Device pick up error on index arm 1. Arm 1 disabled for production.


 


 

Action

Height calibration RETRY+START, arm 1 site D unable to pick up device.

Found Arm 1 site D no vacuum.

Without SLK, no vacuum.

Index arm entry of air tubing, no vacuum.

Check entry of air tubing to vacuum unit, OK.

Check output of air from vacuum unit, no vacuum.

Found gasket at filter of vacuum unit site D, not in groove and causing leak from filter.

Removed filter, fit gasket in groove, tightened and no vacuum leak.

HOT @ 100 degrees, 70% speed cycle run dummy units. No pick up error. Released to production.


 


 

Cause

Found gasket at filter of vacuum unit site D, not in groove and causing leak from filter.

Service History

Subject: NEC TR-60

Handler Model: TR60 (S/N:121175) NEC

Controller: RC520 ( S/N: 01-20947) 3 DU

Date: 3~4 Aug 2009


 

Symptom

Problem 1 - Device Stuck on Socket issue.

 

Problem 2: Device pickup on Soak Boat. Error frequency increased after adjustment for Index Vacuum threshold to -50KPa. Randomly Arm 1 and 2 have device pickup error. But mostly on Arm 1 Head C.

 

Problem 3: Device drop issue. Randomly Arm 1/2 have device drop. But mostly on Arm 1 Head C.

 


 

Action

Problem 1 - Device Stuck on Socket issue.

Remedy: Found index vacuum unit threshold set too low -35KPa. Changed to -50KPa to prevent any poorly pickup device contact to socket.

Situation: Last 2 days no device stuck on socket issue.

 

Problem 2: Device pickup on Soak Boat. Error frequency increased after adjustment for Index Vacuum threshold to -50KPa. Randomly Arm 1 and 2 have device pickup error. But mostly on Arm 1 Head C.

 

Remedy: Fine-tuned Index pickup height on Soak Boat. Making sure Index Arm 1/2 pickup height not too low.(Pressing too much on Soak Boat, prevent Soak Boats from jumping as may lead to devices flip on Tester Buffer). Found out Arm 1 head C contactor blade and suction cup inside contactor blade worn out. Replaced with new contactor blade and suction cup from TSSOP 14P contactor. Verfied vacuum pickup with device, managed to achieve -75KPa, previously -67KPa.

Situation: Improved for Cold TSSOP 8P -40¡„C and TSSOP 8P Active Ambient 25¡„C setup file.

 

Problem 3: Device drop issue. Randomly Arm 1/2 have device drop. But mostly on Arm 1 Head C.

 

Situation: Improved after changed new contactor blade and suction cup on Arm 1 Head C. Customer to moinitor and advised to check/replace suction cups inside contactors blade in case of any device drop on other contactors.


 


 

Cause


 

Remarks

3 Aug 2009

1. Check index vacuum units' vacuum level with device pickup. Vacuum level satisfactory( -67KPA to -83KPa)

2. Check threshold level for index vacuum units. Found out threshold level too low(-35KPa), adjusted to -50KPa.

3. Intial problem was device stuck in socket. Device pickup error on soak boats error frequency increase due to vacuum threshold increased.

4. Fine-tuned index pickup height on soak boats(Tester Buffer). Ensure both index arms' height not pressing hard on soak boat to minimise device flip on soak boats(Tester Buffer). Fine-tuned Picker arm release height on soak boat(Input Buffer).

5. All the above items carried out on TSSOP 8P Cold -43DegC. After fine-tuned, device stuck on socket issue resolved. However, Index Arm 1 Head C frequently have device pickup  and device drop errors.


 

4 Aug 2009

1. Feedback from customer, handler having a lot of device stuck and drop error overnight after they changed setup to TSSOP 8P Active Ambient.

2. Fine-tuned index arm pickup height on soak boat(Tester Buffer) and Picker arm release height on Soak boat(Input Buffer).

3. Handler error frequency minimise, but Arm 1 Head C often have device pick up and device drop error.

4. Disassembled Arm 1 head C contactor, and found out suction cup inside already worn out. Replaced suction cup from TSSOP 14P Changekit and replaced with new contactor blade.

5. Verified vacuum level when pickup device improved from -67 to -75KPa. Device sitting firmly on contactor blade.

6. Monitor handler performance, Arm 1 Head C ®C OK. Advised customer to check/replace if any other contactors having device pickup or drop error.

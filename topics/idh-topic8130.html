Service History

Subject:  NS-7080W EP regulator problem and Output hand 'A' bent.

Handler Model: NS-7080W (S/N: 121282)

Controller: RC520

Date: 30 Dec 2008


 

Symptom

NS-7080W 121282 (46W) at SCS - EP regulator problem and Output hand 'A' bent.


 

Action

After replacing EP regulator (S0341), monitored for 1 hour and there was no air discharge from the compliance unit (handler 'OFF').

Prior to installing hand 'A', it was noticed that the structure of the new part is laterally inverted as compared to the old hand 'A' and it looks like this part is meant for hand 'B' or 'C'. After much verification, it is confirmed that this is a wrong part. 

Had already informed Winston on this issue (S0340) of wrong warranty part sent from Japan. Awaiting for new part arrival.


 

Cause


 

Remarks

SPEL Output to trigger EP regulator:


 

NS-6000: Output Bit 0 to 9 in DU1

NS-7000: Output Bit 0 to 9 in DU1

NS-8000: Output Bit 240 to 249

Subject: NS-8080 "Illegal" Power Off

Handler Model: NS-8080 (S/N: 181677)

Controller: RC520

Date: 29 Jan 2010


 

Symptom

SCS encounter 2 problem with one of their NS8080 Handler.

Software Version: HMI vM9.10-SSY-sp / 3.11ds


 

Fault 1: The handler performed an "illegal" power off during production. The handler automatically power off at the control panel and the whole system stop. The hot mode (100 deg) was switch to ambient mode (50 deg) and the temperature display (Hotplates, Shuttles and chamber) at the HMI shows 50 deg. According to the event log, no power off entry was shown which mean input not triggered (rule out the possiblilties of accidentally push of E.Stop or power off button). No "illegal" power off was encounterd during today production..


 

Had tried out on the other NS8080 regarding the temperature. In normal case, when power off button was depressed, hot mode (100 deg) will switch to ambient mode (50 deg) but the temperature display will still shows the current reading (100 deg). After depressing the power on button, temperature will shows the temperature drop or ambient reading at Shuttles and Chamber.


 


 

Fault 2: Whenever the temperature setting was changed or disable/enable of sites, "setting temperature changed" message was shown in the event log. Disable/enable of sites should be share the same message as temperature setting.

 


 


 

Action

Hidaka requested to collect the following data:

correct these files as follows?

-Select "Manage"

Service History

Subject: UTAC - NS8080-01 output shuttle1 sensor detection error

Handler Model: NS-8080 8 Hands High UPH

Controller: RC520

Date: 15 Dec 2009


 

Symptom

UTAC - NS8080-01 output shuttle1 sensor detection error.


 


 

Action

1) Found out device floating on Y-direction on output shuttle when error occur.

2) Increased counter air turn off and rise from 0.1sec to 0.3 in HMI. No improvement

 Reverted to 0.1sec.

3) Increase index arm1 release by another 1.5mm.

4) Monitor online handler performance, release to customer and advise customer to monitor.


 


 

Cause

Maybe Index device release height to output shuttle is too low and device could be light.

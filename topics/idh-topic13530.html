Service History

Subject: SCB NS-7160W Support for DB changekit 48QFN 6x6 16sites setup

Handler Model: NS-7160W

Controller: RC520

Date: 21~22 Dec 2010


 

Symptom

SCB NS-7160W Support for DB changekit 48QFN 6x6 16sites setup.


 


 

Action

1.   Inspected C/K condition.

2.    Setup 48QFN 6x6 package.

3.    Triggered Index Arms' vacuum and checked vacuum levels w/o device. Found Arm 1 site 3 and 4/ Arm 2 site 8 and 16 vacuum low(-11KPa) comparing to the rest(-24KPa). Increased vacuum level for affected sites.

4.    Triggered counter air for index arm, found out arm 2 site 2 purge air too strong as compare to the rest. Reduce air purge volume by comparing with the rest of the sites.

5.    Carried out pick and place alignment on ambient.

6.    Heated up to 130ºC, and carried out pick and place alignment.

7.    Encountered software bug. Input Arm open/close in open position after retry(reload tray at loader) after ¡° No input tray¡± operator call happened. Causing Input Arm Hand A unable to pickup unit on try. Unable to recover with retry or Home. Only can recover by clean out.

8.    Adjusted loader, empty1 and empty 2 cylinder lifter for middle position as finger clamp to tray during transportation.

9.    Increased Input/Output Counter air regulator to 0.3Mpa according to factor spec. Found regulator set as 0.2Mpa resulting low vacuum pickup when pick up device(Before range -67KPa to -73KPa). After adjustment vacuum range from -75KPa to -87KPa)


 

1.    Reteach Shuttle 1 P21 loading and P23 unloading position as seen index arm1 caused shuttle shift position during pickup and placement on shuttle 1.

2.    Changed to rubber suction cups as requested by customer.

3.    Carried out hand height calibration on both input/ output Arms

4.    Carried out pick and place alignment.

5.   Completed 4000units changekit verification in Hot mode 130ºc according to Statschippac's specification.

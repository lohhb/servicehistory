Subject: NS-8080 3x3 device Input Shuttle device floating

Handler Model: NS-8080

Controller: RC520

Date: 10 Jul 2010


 

Symptom

STM Muar 3x3 Input Shuttle device floating issue, suspect device in tray pocket to be NG.


 


 

Action

Possible causes due:

1) tray pocket tolerance

2) device may be displaced easily

3) loader cylinder tray down motion may be too fast/hard

4) conveyor tray in motion may be too fast/hard

5) tray pusher may be too hard and warp tray causing device jump during pick and place.


 


 

Cause

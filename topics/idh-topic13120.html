Service History

Subject: STM NS-8080 MKS controller -100+v during HOT mode

Handler Model: NS-8080

Controller: RC520

Date: 13 Aug 2010


 

Symptom

STM NS-8080 MKS controller -100+v during HOT mode.

Found value to start rising when power to heater supplied and drop when power cut.

Suspect noise from CB5 power of heater.


 

Action

Use aluminum to shield small area of sensor cable and stop at -11v.

When aluminum removed, rise up steadily to -80v.

To feedback to EPSON and MKS on findings - Hidaka-san


 

Cause

Noise caused ESD to increase.

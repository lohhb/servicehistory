Service History

Subject: NS-7000 Rotator Hands upgrade

Handler Model: NS-7000

Controller: RC520

Date: 2~3 May 2013 and 15 May 2013


 

Symptom

SCS NS7000 Rotator Upgrade.


 


 

Action

2~3 May 2013 (11 Hrs)

- Backup SPEL System.
- Removed input/output open-close arm.
- Installed input/output rotator arm
- Vacuum and counter air check.
- setting of config.
- functional check using spel.
- running check carried out.
- Hand pitch adjustment carried out.
- Auto teaching carried out.
- Fine tuning for all unloaders and fix tray.


 

15 May 2013 (8 Hrs)

- Disconnect all input/output arms wiring and tubing
- Remove input/output arms.
- Fit up rotator arms
- Connect all the wiring and tubing.
- Vacuum and counter air check.
- Height calibration carried out.
- Fine tuning carried out for all unloaders.

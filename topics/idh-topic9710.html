Service History

Subject: TR-60 Unable to Home

Handler Model: TR-60

Controller: RC520

Date: 27 Feb 2009


 

Symptom

T-4302 The Pulse Generating Board detected an alarm signal. Manipulator 10, Joint 1.

T-4014 MCAL is not completed. Manipulator 10, Joint 1.

T-4046 Communication error with the Pulse Generating Board. Manipulator 10, Joint 1.

In buffer's robot 10 unable to complete MCAL in SPEL. Found slit plate for HOME sensor of robot 10 tilted, which lead to unable to find home position.


 

Action

For In buffer, Robot 10, reposition for slit plate to a horizontal position, able to home.


 


 

Cause

In buffer, Robot 10, slit plate position tilt causing robot unable to cut Home sensor.


 

Remarks

PG #1(Hand A and B up/down for Input & Output Hands)

PG #2(For Soak Robot 8, 9, 10 & 11)

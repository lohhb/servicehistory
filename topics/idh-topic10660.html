Service History

Subject: NEC NS-7000 Robot controller (SRC520) error occurs on Input Arm, Output Arm, Shuttle 1 and Shuttle 2.

Handler Model: NS-7000

Controller: RC520

Date: 3~5 Aug 2009


 

Symptom

Problem: Robot controller (SRC520) error occurs on Input Arm, Output Arm, Shuttle 1 and Shuttle 2.

Mostly on Shuttle 2 have torque error in low power state. Problem occurred, after customer replaced x02 bearings slider on Shuttle 2 linear guide w/o calibration and teaching on loading/unloading points.


 


 

Action

After calibration and teaching done, problem still exist. Disabled Shuttle 2, overnight running on 03/08/09 no robot controller error.

Root cause still unknown, as shuttle 2 motion on linear guide still N.G as may lead to Servo overload A, Servo overload B on Shuttle 2 and torque error on Input/Otput Arm. See Attached SRC Error history.

 

Situation: Temporary running with shuttle 2 disable. Advised customer to replaced Shuttle 2 linear guide and perform proper calibration and teaching. KGS to provide solution to NECSEM when received advises from EPSON.


 

Note: Even if Index Arm 2 is disabled by Shuttle selection, With Device, during or after One Cycle, Index Arm 2 will still go down to test site to check for device in socket.

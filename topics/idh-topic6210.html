Service History

Subject: EEMS Output Shuttle Device Condition Error
Handler Model: NS-7000(EEMS, S/N:181259 - NS70-27)

Controller: RC520

Date: 7 Mar 2008


 

Symptom

During production, encounter output shuttle device condition error.

A device on the Output Shuttle is missing during the transport.


 

Action

-Do auto contact height calibration.

-Reduce the counter air on time

-Decrease the counter air (Index air regulator)

 Production monitoring for 5X5 package carried ok


 


 

Cause


 


 

Remarks

Check points:

1) pickup head(rubber or semitron) any wear and tear, rubber/semitron pickup head should protrude abit out from nest surface

2) Nest condition(include o-ring, spring and plunger condition), clean the nest, spring and plunger.

3) Output shuttle sensor position(adjust if required) and wiring

4) Check counter air whether too strong or weak with/without nest/layout kit/direct on index arm

5) Index Counter Air On time

6) Counter air volume on Air regulator

7) Individual counter air volume on vacuum switch

8) Device dimension(try dummy device run)

9) Swap layout kit

10) Reduce or increase the device release height to output shuttle

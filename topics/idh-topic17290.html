Subject: NS-7080W HDD Recovery and chamber's PT sensor replacement

Handler Model: NS-7080W (S/N: 121401, NS7KW-08)

Controller: RC520 (S/N: 01-25413)

Date: 15 Sep 2011


 

Symptom

Xilinx  – NS7080W HDD recovery and chamber PT sensor replacement.


 


 

Action

-Chamber PT sensor replaced and functional check carried out.
-System backup
-HDD recovery carried out.
-Backup files restored individually and timing of system boot up was taken whenever a backup file was restored.
-Height calibration carried out.
-During dummy run, several devices unable to pickup at Index Arm 1.
- Found Index Arm 1 plunged too low when picking up devices at Shuttle 1, so pickup height increase by about 1mm.

-Chamber monitor connector broken on NS7080W-06 SN:121040
an NS7080W SN:121298.
-Chamber monitor connector replacement. X2

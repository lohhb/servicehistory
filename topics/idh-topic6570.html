Subject: NS-7080W Output arm, Hand B got device pick up error at Output shuttle 1 & 2

Handler Model: NS-7080W(S/N: 121289, 49W, StatsChippac)

Controller: RC520 (MMI v7.10-SXC, SPEL CT 2.81b1H)

Date: 11 Apr 2008 (Buyoff completed: 14 Apr 2008)


 

Symptom

Problem met while doing fine-tuning for LQFP 14x14 package :

Sometimes, Output arm, Hand B got device pick up error at Output shuttle 1 & 2.


 

Action

Even though pick up offset was decreased from -0.3 to -1.5 still to no avail (sometimes can pick up, but sometimes cannot), but tried “Home”,”Retry”,”Start”, then pick up was good always. Found out that this may be due to the spring structure on the individual hand.  Rightfully, the spring should sit in a groove on the pulley system, but due to the hand movement, sometimes the spring did not sit in, thus the hand is higher, so cannot pick up the device.  When the output arm cover was opened, noticed that the spring was not sit in the groove.


 


 

Cause

When the output arm cover was opened, noticed that the spring was not sit in the groove.

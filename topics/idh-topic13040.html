Service History

Subject: NS-8040 Index Arm 1 Site B some pins for device under test open circuit(Bin 5)

Handler Model: NS-8040(NS-006)

Controller: RC520

Date: 22 Jul 2010


 

Symptom

Index Arm 1 Site B some pins for device under test open circuit(Bin 5).


 


 

Action

Index Arm 1 alignment check by Index Arm Alignment jig, found Head 3/4 co-planarity no good, 0.02mm shim able to fit in.

Head 3/4 co-planarity adjustment from bottom of Head 3/4 bolts.


 

Metal bracket(with door magnet) came off from side door of Index area.

Secure back the 2 bolts that hold the metal bracket.


 


 

Cause

Index Arm 1 alignment out for Head 3/4.

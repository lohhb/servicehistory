Service History

Subject: NS-7000 Encounter Database Open Error during startup

Handler Model: NS-7000 (SMART MODULE)

Controller: RC520

Date: 1 Mar 2011


 

Symptom

Encountered Database Open Error during startup.

Tester trip numerous time leading to improper shutdown/application of NS7000.


 


 

Action

1)   Handler does not have USB so unable to copy Hander.mdb file by floppy.

2)   Locate Handler.mdb and delete. Re-install MMI.

3)   During HOME, encounter SRC error.

4)   Found linear slide to be very rough and bad condition.

5)   SMART does not have EPSON grease. Grease to best condition and advise SMART to purchase linear slide and proper grease.

6)   After grease, able to HOME.

7)   During height calibration, encounter index reach the limit of motion range.

8)   Suspect z and u axis range too small. Changed from -49000 to -60000.

9)   During height calibration, found arm 2 site G “purge device”

10) Found vacuum sensor flickering causing vacuum cut and device drop. Adjust sensitivity. OK.

11)             Cycle run and no more problems. Handler release for production.

Service History

Subject:  Index Arm 1-A found with flying device(Device condition error)

Handler Model: NS-7000 (S/N: 18825)

Controller: RC520

Date: 4 Jun 2009


 

Symptom

During buyoff, Index Arm 1-A found with flying device QFN 9x9 (Device condition error) after Index Arm 1 unload device to output shuttle 1.


 


 

Action

Initially, counter air valve was adjusted but to no avail.  Then, counter air on time was set slightly higher also to no avail.  Tubing (x3) on counter air control unit was swapped from contactor A to contactor B, but still have the same problem.  Therefore, suspect the control unit is malfunction. Going a further step by checking the set screw on the counter air control unit found that it was not open at all, thus, adjusted and counter air on contactor A was good.


 


 

Cause

Set screw on Index Counter Air Control Unit (valve for counter air) was closed causing device unable to purge out.

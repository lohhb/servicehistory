Subject: Xilinx NS-7080W(NS7KW-06) Index Arm 1 alignment by Index Test Hand Teaching jig and Index Arm Teaching jig.

Handler Model: NS-7080W (NS7KW-06, S/N: 121040)

Controller: RC520

Date: 30 Jul 2009


 

Symptom

Xilinx NS-7080W(NS7KW-06) Index Arm 1 alignment by Index Test Hand Teaching jig and Index Arm Teaching jig.


 


 

Action

Index Arm 1 & 2 checking by Index Test Hand Teaching jig(latched at Index Arm) and Index Arm Teaching jig(fixed at test area) at p50 and p51.

 Check distance between Index Arm1's  Hand plate(H01-B150-001) and Index Arm 2's Hand plate(H01-B150-001), was about 39.5mm.

 EPSON specification is 40mm.

Customer informed that Index Arm 2 is okay but Index Arm 1 caused shred BGA balls.

 In this case, we will not make any adjustment to Index Arm 2.

At p50, Arm 2 latched with Index Test Hand Teaching  jig and sit into input shuttle 2.

 At p50, for Arm 1, loosen 4 bolts on top of Hand support(H01-B150-002), make Arm1 sit into Index Arm Teaching jig and slowly tighten the 4 bolts.

At p51, check Arm 2 sit into Index Arm Teaching jig, Arm 1 latched with Index Test Hand Teaching  jig and sit into input shuttle 1.
 Dummy devices run, ok.

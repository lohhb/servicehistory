Service History

Subject: NS-7080W Error F-5041 and F-5047

Handler Model: NS-7080W (NS7KW-01, Xilinx)

Controller: RC520

Date: 29 Apr 2008


 

Symptom

XAP NS7KW-01 F-5041 and F-5047 follow-up.

F-5041 Torque error in the Low power state.

F-5047 Overspeed in the Low power state.


 

Action

MCD file is for error T-4241 “The detected speed at low power state is too fast”.

F-5041 and F-5047 requires Manipulator Tunning application.

T-4241 The detected speed at the Low power state is too fast.

1)In SPEL, normal jump P1 and P2 in LP (Low Power) already encountered “overspeed in low power state” error.

2)Found slight vibration during this error when tray transporter stop moving.

3)Original Low Power Overspeed ratio at 1.2kpps

4)Changed to 2.0kpps for monitoring. 1 hour production no problem.

5)When handler is available for offline check, will check hardware condition like motor,

timing bel tension etc.

6)Handler still running production and unable to tune w.r.t servo data. Probably,

servo settling data maybe able to reduce vibration.

Controller: RC520

Date: 22 Mar 2010


 

Q&A

Informations NS-8040 Ionizers KD-750B(TOP BLOWER IONIZERS) & KD-740B-EP(TEST SITE) for NS-8040.


 


 

1) When fan stop for Ionizers KD-750B(top blower ioizers) or KD-740B-EP(test site), will the NS-8040 detect fan stop, give alarm and stop?


 

Ans: Yes.


 

2) When there is an ion imbalance for KD-750B(top blower ioizers) or KD-740B-EP(test site),  ( either too much -ve ions or too much +ve ions), will the NS-8040 detect imbalance of ions, give alarm and stop?


 

Ans:

There is no any detection about Ion balance.

However, it has cleaning alarm.

When the balance is big different, there will be alarm.


 

 


 

3) If ion imbalance can be detected, then what is the range(eg -/+ 5V, -/+ 10V, -/+ 12V)?


 

Ans:

There is no any ion balance detection


 

Note: Cleaning alarm

         This is detected by the amount of electric discharge on the needle

         It is related decay capacity.


 

         When it will be alarm?

         For KD-750B: Over 3 times decay capacity


 

         For KD-740B: Over 2 times decay capacity


 

          For reference:

         Convert to ion balance:

          Around +/- 30 to 40 V

         (It is depended on environments. 

Therefore, there is no guarantee the above value. )

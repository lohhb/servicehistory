Service History

Subject:

a) Device Condition Error at Output Shuttle 1 pocket D

b) Misplacement at Auto1-3 and Fix1

Handler Model: NS-7000 (S/N: 181322, NS70-36, EEMS)

Controller: RC520, MMI 2.0j, SPEL CT 2.81b1H

Date:


 

Symptom

EEMS NS70-36:

a) Device Condition Error at Output Shuttle 1 (Index 1 Head D).

b) Misplacement at Auto1-3 and Fix1.


 


 

Action

1) Swapping the Index 1 contact head D with C but problem didn't migrate over.

2) Adjusting of Index Arm Counter Air regulator solve regularly error.

3) By increase and decrease of release height did not solve the problem.

4) Adjusting of individual counter air and increase of counter air time resolve the problem after monitoring for a few trays.

5) Re-align of Auto1-3 and Fix1.


 


 

Cause

a) Device Condition Error at Output Shuttle 1 pocket D.

=>Adjusting of Index Arm Counter Air regulator solve regularly error.

=>Adjusting of individual counter air on the vacuum unit and increase of counter air time resolve the problem.


 

b) Misplacement at Auto1-3 and Fix1. => Re-align of Auto1-3 and Fix1.

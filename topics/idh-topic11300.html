Service History

Subject: NS-3000 GPIB communication error

Handler Model:

Controller:

Date: 28 Feb 2011, 2~4 & 8 Mar 2011


 

Symptom

AMD Penang encounter GPIB communications error.

They used tester GPIB card and installed driver. However, it is not compatible. Lead to ocx timestamp error.


 


 

Action

28 Feb 2011

Recovery CD was used to recover without consultation.

Missing drivers, MMI software etc. AMD(S) to prepare a HDD with full installations to be sent to Penang.

Phone support to setup new HDD.

2~4 Mar 2011

AMD Penang received spare HDD from AMD Singapore. Still unable to communicate.

Tester National Instrument(NI) software failed to detect presence of NS3000 GPIB.

Chuah dispatch 03 March.

IFUNT Unit led display able to display according to tester type selection.

Able to load and verify HEX file with no error.

However, tester NI still unable to detect GPIB.

To ship IFUNT100 board SKP-383 and SKP-369-1 for verification.


 

8 Mar 2011

Received SKP383 and SKP369-1 board with full cables.

AMD replaced SKP383 board, able to initialize with tester.

AMD to issue PO for service + SKP383.


 


 

Cause

Faulty IFUNT100A board SKP383.

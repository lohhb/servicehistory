Service History

Subject: NS-8080 8 Hands output Arm hand up sensor doesn't turn on

Handler Model: NS-8080 (NS8080-01, S/N: 181665) 8 Hands High UPH

Controller: RC520

Date:12~14 Jan 2011


 

Symptom

UTAC  NS8080-01(181665) output arm hand up sensor doesn't turn on issue.


 


 

Action

Previously replaced Control board SKP478 on 23/12/10.

Encountered same issue again after 3 weeks.

Found output arm Distribution board SKP479_2B no led light during running. Replaced with spare board and monitored production.

Was informed by Ray problem came back again at 1030pm.


 

13 Jan 2011

Check all cables and connectors, found one cable sleeve damaged. Replaced with spare.

Re-sit all cable connectors to boards and z-axis module.

Dry cycle handler and push in for production.

Under monitoring.


 

14 Jan 2011

Checked handler alarm history and no occurrence of hand up sensor doesn't turn on error. Handler still in production as of 7pm.

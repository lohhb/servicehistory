Service History

Subject: AMD ATC installation to NS-3000 (NS303)

Handler Model: NS-3000 (NS303, S/N: 15770)

Controller: RC520

Date: 3~10 Sep 2009


 

Symptom

AMD ATC installation for NS-3000 (NS303).


 


 

Action

3 Sep 2009

Removed Index Unit top Alu and Rear Alu long bracket.

Temporarily tie cable tie to Index Arm 2 Up/Down linear slide pack, remove side Alu metal bracket from 2 slider packs.

Drill 2 M3 holes on Alu metal bracket and use tap to tap M3 screw holes.

Secure straight-flat metal to Alu metal bracket with M3x10 (2 pcs) bolts.

Install back Alu metal bracket to Index Arm 2.

Remove existing 3 Kasuga ionizers from NS-3000.

Remove perpex cover for Index Arm 2.

Installed Head #1, #2, #5 & #6 extension cable to the top back metal trunking of handler and through the FIX tray and handler base.

The  Return& Supply hoses insert through oval shape hole perpex on top of handler.

Attached Head #5 and #6 to Index Arm 2, secure Return& Supply hose and ATC Head #5,#6 cable using to cable ties tied to the straight-flat metal. Cable ties temporarily hold the cables and hose.

For Index Arm 2, attached 3 Alu spaces with 3 pcs of plastic cable holder and hold the  Return& Supply hose and ATC Head #1,#2cable. There was no cable tie base to hold cables and hose at the top of the handler, temporarily secure by cable tie.

Check Index Arm 2 Return& Supply hose and ATC Head #5,#6cable condition at Input shuttle 2 pickup position, P71:Z(0):U(0), P70:Z(0):U(0) and contact to socket position by ensuring no rubbing to ATC cables.

Without the cable tie base, it was difficult to secure the  Return& Supply hose and ATC Head #5,#6cable.

BH Wee took the black manifold for Return & Supply(for chiller side) and will return on Monday.


 

4 Sep 3009

Solder wiring connector for DIO 3 connector.

Attached big cable tie base and temporarily secure Return& Supply hose and ATC Head #1,#2, #5 and #6 cable.

Check Index Arm 1 Return& Supply hose and ATC Head #1,#2cable condition at Input shuttle 1 pickup position, P71:Z(0):U(0), P70:Z(0):U(0) and contact to socket position by ensuring no rubbing to ATC cables.

Check Index Arm 2 Return& Supply hose and ATC Head #5,#6cable condition at Input shuttle 2 pickup position, P71:Z(0):U(0), P70:Z(0):U(0) and contact to socket position by ensuring no rubbing to ATC cables.


 

7 Sep 2009

Remove chiller,  PS9600 power unit and four temperature controllers from cartons and mount to handler.

Connect  PS96000 power supply cables to the four temperature controller units

Connect DIO3 connector to DU3. From DIO3 connect P6 Black wire to Blue terminal block P6 of handler Power supply box, connect N1 Black wire to Green terminal block N1 of handler Power supply box. Connect Temp Ready cables from DIO3 to temperature controllers.

Install Contec COM-4(LPCI)H RS232 board to RC520.

Refer to 0006-NS3K for installing drivers and reserve hardware resources in  Windows 2000, in SPEL Hardware I/O board changing IFUNT100 I/O address and CONTEC I/O board address.


 


 

9 Sep 2009

Due to wrong ATC software from Hidaka-san, HDD corrupted. Tried to uninstall wrong software and install software Ver A9.11-AMD (07/10/2009, FD09S075) but encounter time stamp error. After remove time stamp error also cannot launch Nstask bar.

Do HDD recovery by using Recovery CD for NS3000 FD05S065 ver3.0 2005/12/14.

Restore backup to NS3000.

Install  ATC software Ver A9.11-AMD.

ATC standalone check on manual setpoint temperature at 25DegC and 40DegC on temperature controller, ok.

However, in SPEL I/O check input bit 40~43 never turn on with ATC temperature controllers connected. Cables and connection check for ATC.

Bring Back DIO3 connector for troubleshooting at getech

10 Sep 2009

DIO3 checking by KuoHong, found one of the corner solder point too much of solder touching cover for DIO3. File away excess solder.


 

Connect DIO3 connector without cover to DU3. From DIO3 connect P6 Black wire to Blue terminal block P6 of handler Power supply box, connect N1 Black wire to Green terminal block N1 of handler Power supply box.

Connect Temp Ready cables from DIO3 to temperature controllers.

SPEL Input I/O bit 40~43, ok all ON(RED) in normal working condition.
 Also check with temperature controller switch off, and that respective Bit
will turn OFF in SPEL I/O.
 Test in ATC MMI, set to high temperature and set point 35DegC and start
handler, the display setpoint on 4 temperature controller changed to 35DegC, ok.
 Put back cover to DIO3 connector, test ATC function again from MMI, ok.

Alignment check with device for NS3000 with ATC at 40DegC for Auto1~Fix4, ok.


 


 

Cause


 

Remarks

Set setpoint temperature in ATC MMI, will send the signal from DIO3 to individual ATC temperature controller to set & control the respective ATC Heat temperature.

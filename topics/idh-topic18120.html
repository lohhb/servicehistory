Subject: NS-8040 ATC replace Thermal Head 5 Straight Fittings and Tubing

Handler Model: NS-8040 ATC (NS80-37)

Controller: RC520

Date: 16 Jan 2012


 

Symptom

UTAC - NS80-37 replace ATC thermal head 5 straight fittings and tubing.


 


 

Action

- Power up handler. Check functionality of ATC chiller and thermal head.
- Thermal head 5 unable to heat up.

Found peltier black wire broken.

No spare peltier to change.


 - Continue replace thermal head straight fittings and tubing.
- Fixed back thermal head to index arm 2, power on chiller and checked that no water leakage.
- Release handler.

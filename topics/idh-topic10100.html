Service History

Subject: NS-7000 Output Arm hang during teaching, cannot do teaching

Handler Model: NS-7000 (S/N: 18724)

Controller: RC520

Date: 28 Apr 2009


 

Symptom

Upon teaching, there is an error in "SPEL error history" whenever the output arm hung during teaching.  And this error is pertaining to Shuttle 1 and Shuttle 2 manipulators out of limit.


 

Action

Found out that the zero pulse for both shuttle 1 and shuttle 2 were out which cause arm robots to behave weird. 

a) Auto Teaching carried out. After Auto teaching at shuttle 1 & 2, found out that the x-axis out by 9mm. Therefore, Shuttle 1 & 2 teaching carried out found that shuttle 1 P21 is a negative value.


 

b) Motor calibration carried out and the P21 value at positive value. Therefore, Shuttle 1 teaching and auto teaching for shuttle 1 & 2 carried out.


 

After re-calibrating the zero pulse for shuttle 1 and 2, teaching was carried out a few times without fail.  All robot teach, heating plate taken from 18825 and installed on shuttle 1.  Heating was good.

System backed up with a problem, that is, there is bad sector in HDD. After "fixdisk",, back up no problem.

Software MMi ver 1.1c10 loaded and dry run carried out found good.

Note: HDD looks like failing, as there is bad sector.

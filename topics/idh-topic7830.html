Service History

Subject: NS7000 EPSON 3, S/N:181098 Rotator Hands upgrade

Handler Model: NS-7000(Unisem Batam)

Controller: Rc520

Date: 22 Oct 2008


 

Symptom


 


 

Action

NS7000 EPSON 3, s/n:181098 rotator arm upgrade.

-remove open/close arm and install rotator arm.

-check vacuum, counter air and sensors condition.

-check alignment using shuttle jig

-edit config.exe option for rotator and hand pitch setting to 80x60.

-height calibration for z height of hands.

-change rotation parameter in SPELCT <CONST.INC> file.

-run 7x7 QFN dummy devices to confirm offset and rotation.


 


 

Removal of Open/Close Arm


 

1)   Label the tubing before removing tubing.

2)   Remove all covers.

3)   Disconnect the all connectors.

4)   Remove the Open/Close Arm by loosen M4 screw x 4.


 


 

Fitment of Rotation Arm


 

1)   Fit up the Rotation Arm (M4 screw x 4).

2)   Align the arm using alignment jig.

3)   Connect and fit up the tubing and connectors.

4)   Turn on the Vacuum and Counter Air of each arm and check the condition. Also check the sensors.

5)   Fix up all the covers.

6)   Change the setting of arm from Open/Close Arm to Rotating Arm and Hand Pitch to 80mm(X-Pitch) and 60mm(Y-Pitch) in Config.exe.

7)   Ensure that the rotation speed is 80%.

8)   Carried out Height Calibration for both Arm.

9)   Carried out alignment check with Dummy Devices.

10)              Change the Parameter as shown below.

a)   Enter <SPEL CT> ? <Abort All> ? <P> ? Click on <CONST.INC> ? <Edit>

b)   Click <Edit> ? <Find…> ? Key in <CKD>

c)   Change the values shown below

CKD_RotationHandSpeed

8550

CKD_RotationHandAccel

333333

CKD_RotationHandDecel

333333

d)   Click <Save> ? <Close>

e)   Click on <CONST.INC> ? <Add>

Service History

Subject: NS-8080 Misplacement

Handler Model: NS-8080 (NS80-03 & NS80-01)

Controller: RC520

Date: 10 Jan 2011


 

Symptom

NS-8080 misplacement.


 


 

Action

1.   STM Muar new tray design of 6 walls for each pocket, has device jump on loader tray and misplacement on auto 1.

2.   NS80-03 is ok but NS80-01 is N.G.

3.   STM Muar NS80-01, found output Hand D is slant condition.

4.   Loosen top screw, able to align back into jig hole, upon tighten, goes back into out position.

5.   Advise Ken to try and insert shim/paper to overcome the slant.

6.   Because misplacement on unloading side always caused by hand D, may be able to reduce misplacement if can align hand D into jig.

7.   Loader tray device jump, found picker hand to hit 6 walls tray.

8.   Raised pickup height, no more device jump.

9.   New 6wall tray found spec to be tighter than 4wall. Initial design of island in pocket, STM Muar will mill off island as this design increases chance of misplacement with 6wall and tighter pocket spec.

14 Jan 2011

Using shim, to align Hand D into shuttle jig.

Customer to PO Hand D as shim is temporary solution.

Found tray pusher of auto 1, unable to push tray to correct position.

Auto 2 and 3, able to always push tray to correct position.

With Hand D aligned, slight misplacement still occurs on auto 1 especially if tray is not in correct position.

Auto 1 and Auto 2 tray pusher swapped. Improved but occasionally still unable to push tray into position.

Increased air of tray pusher on auto 1, sometimes still unable to push tray to correct position.

Found tray stopper on auto 1 slightly tilted. Align back but sometimes, still unable to push tray to correct position.

Tray guide gap is found within spec of 1 metal ruler thickness.

STM Muar will order side tray pusher as permanent solution.

Although NS80-03 does not have this problem, STM Muar will make it handler standard config. Additional side pusher for loader and auto 1.


 


 

Cause

Misplacement due to

1)New design tray problem, 6 walls with tray pocket tighter tolerance.

2)NS80-01 Hand D slant

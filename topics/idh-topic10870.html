Handler Model: NS-8040 (NS-001)

Controller: RC520

Date: 16 Sep 2009


 

Symptom

Emitter does not emit light for Input Shuttles Device floating sensor.

Infineon NS-8040 input shuttle sensor having some problem.

Measured incoming for SKP456B sensor board @ infineon which is 25.9V and office NS-8040 is 23.4V.

Do you have any idea where can we adjust this 24V supply? From PSU1 or PSU2? as in wiring diagram didn't mention.

 

Tried to replace another sensor board SKP456B, but same issue with when plug in SQ120 sensor to board X12connector and SQ112 to board X13 connector, sensors get killed(Emitter becomes very bright after connector plugged in then emitter blackout).

For those working sensors' components ZD measuring 9.3kΩ.

Sensor SQ120, SQ112 and SQ121 and SQ122 damaged by SKP456B sensor board.

Action


 


 

Cause

EPSON informed that zener diode shorted on SKP456B caused the emitter to burnt.

Remedy is to cut(remove) the zener diode before it becomes shorted.

18 Feb 2010 email, sensor problem for NS-8040 and NS-8080H. The shuttle sensor sometimes breaks by the failure of the ZD diode on  the substrate circuit.

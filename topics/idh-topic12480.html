Subject: NS-8080 Index Arm Octal sites devices stuck on contactor Head after test

Handler Model: NS-8080(NS88-04; S/N: 181655) 8 Hands High UPH

Controller: RC520

Date: 30 Apr 2010


 

Symptom

Index Arm Octal sites devices stuck on contactor Head  after test.


 


 

Action

1) Trigger Index Arm 1 & 2 vacuum, found vacuum level for Arm1 (at low vaccum)

2) Trimmed down vacuum level to -12kpa

3) Set release height to shuttle to 2mm above pickup from shuttle

4) In HMI for Index Arm, activate "tun off and rise" from 0.1 sec to 0.2 sec

5) Monitor handler.ok

Service History

Subject: UTAC  Reported by customer index vacuum unit faulty on NS80-24

Handler Model: NS-8040

Controller: RC520

Date: 25 Nov 2009


 

Symptom

Reported by customer index vacuum unit faulty on NS80-24.


 


 

Action

1) Observed P2 LED indicator always on.

2) Check vacuum unit settings.

3) Found P1, P2, H and F setting as -50, 6, 10 and F1.

4) Revert back to original factory settings as -40, 6, 0 and F0.

5) Verified vacuum unit OK.


 


 

Cause

Wrong setting of P1 and P2 for Index Vacuum Switch Unit.

Symptom

Xilinx  NS7KW-08 shuttle 1 vibration, caused pick and place errors.

Patch MCSYS05.MCD file.

Check Index P51 to shuttle 1 P23 unloading position, reteach P23 unloading position from 382.892 to 383.206.

Perform height cal in 100 degree hot mode.

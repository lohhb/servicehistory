Service History

Subject: UTAC  NS8080-05 frequent servo error on Output Arm Hand B

Handler Model: NS-8080 (NS8080-05) 8 Hands High UPH

Controller: RC520

Date: 14 Dec 2010


 

Symptom

UTAC NS8080-05 frequent servo error on Output Arm Hand B.


 


 

Action

Off panel power and check every individual hands of both input/ output arm.

Apply oil to all hands.

Found out Input Arm Hand H and Output Hand B rough movement manually by hand.

Cycle run handler.

Advise customer to monitor performance.

Trigger Warranty replacement for Input Arm Hand H and Output Arm Hand B.( Warranty Ref: S0459 and S0460)

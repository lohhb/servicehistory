Subject: How to get the average reading on MKS Novx 3360?

Handler Model:

Controller:

Date: 1 Jul 2011


 

Symptom

How to get the average reading on MKS Novx 3360 for ESD Class 0.


 


 

Action

Current settings of calibrator reader: Display Avg = 10, Polls/sec = 25.

Which means MKS Novx 3360 controller will calculate and display the average out of 10 readings captured.

And 0.4 sec used to capture 1 reading. So there maybe a possibility that some peak value not captured during this time frame.


 

Action: According to Simco engineer, change the display avg to ‘0’, so that any possible change in the reading will be captured. To carry out this change and monitor.

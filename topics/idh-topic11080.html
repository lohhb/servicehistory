Subject: UTAC  NS80-21 and NS80-22 socket pins worn out issue

Handler Model: NS-8040

Controller: RC520

Date: 9 Nov 2009


 

Symptom

UTAC  NS80-21 and NS80-22 socket pins worn out issue.


 


 

Action

Found socket floating check disable in unit set index arm.

So even if index arm over plunge and cut floating sensor, there will be no alarm.

Also found index arm floating sensors height difference and some of sensor bracket holder bent.

Advise customer to reteach sensor position.

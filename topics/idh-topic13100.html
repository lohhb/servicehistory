Service History

Subject: After rotator stages installed to NS-8080, encounter intermittent power trip

Handler Model: NS-8080 (NS8080-02, S/N: 181464)

Controller: RC520

Date: 2~3 Aug 2010


 

Symptom

After rotator stages installed to NS-8080, encounter intermittent power trip.


 

Action

Narrow down to output rotator stage. Will swap in stepper driver card to verify.


 

Replaced driver card from 181523


 

13 Aug 2010

Replace back stepper driver from NS8080-02 rotator upgrade which was suspected to cause trip. Dry cycle run for 1hr no problem. Release handler.


 


 


 

Cause

According to Hidaka, the power trip due to this NS-8080 is old type so the 24V power supper unable to provide sufficient power to Input and Output Rotator Stage.

Need to replace to another type of 24V power supply.

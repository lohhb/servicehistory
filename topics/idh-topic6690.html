Service History

Subject: NS-8080 Temperature Error

Handler Model: NS-8080 (STATSChippac)

Controller: RC520

Date: 27 Jun 2008


 

Symptom

During the confirmation run on NS8080 for 3 lot of devices, "CH6 temperature control error (chamber)" occurred as the chamber temperature rise above the guard band (92.1°C)(JAM1523).

"Retry", "Clean Out" & "Alarm reset" buttons were light up on the control panel.

When the "Clean out" button was depressed instead of "Retry" button, the whole system would hang up but showing "Running" status and the all the temperature start to decrease.

"Pause","Reset" and "Start" buttons have to be depressed to get back the actual temperature.


 


 

Action

Temperature stablilizer plate installed to stablise temperature.


 


 

Cause


 

Remarks

EPSON requested to install Stabilization plate for Temperature fluctuate issue

 then on below systems.
 NS8080 #133 181415
 NS8080 #134 181416
 NS8080 #135 181417


 

4 Aug 2008

STATSChippac, after installing the stabilizer plate on the NS8080 for about a week, there's still some temperature fluctuation at the Chamber.


 

10 Aug 2008

For EEMS, after installation of stablilzer plate on NS8080, 30th July 08 to 5th August 08 and found that there was a few occurrences on the chamber temperature fluctuation again.


 

Dec 2008

EPSON consider as high priority to modify hardware for temperature fluctuation in the Chamber.

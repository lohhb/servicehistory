Subject: ASE – Verifications of Index Arm's P50 and P51 teaching point and coplanarity

Handler Model: NS-8080 (H01-NS88 s/n: 181618)

Controller: RC520

Date: 18 Feb 2011


 

Symptom

ASE – Verifications of Index Arm's P50 and P51 teaching point and coplanarity.


 


 

Action

As inform by Terry, Arm 2 have frequent device drop after test at site B, D and F.

1)    Verified index arm 1 and 2 Point 50 and 51 using index arm alignment jig. OK

2)    Check coplanarity for Arm 2, found gap at 1 corner using 0.02mm and 0.05mm gap. Realigned Arm 2 coplanarity, managed to improve, only 0.02mm gap at 1 corner.

3)    Swapped Arm 1 layout kit to Arm 2 and Arm 2 layout kit to Arm 1.

4)    Verified with units, no device drop in 100% speed.

5)    Swapped back layout kit back.

6)    Found device drop always have device drop after test.

7)   Informed Terry to ask K.A to verify layout kits.

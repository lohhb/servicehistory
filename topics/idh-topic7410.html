Subject: Kasuga KD-750B ionizer fan cleaning and stop alarm

Handler Model: NS-8040(EEMS)

Controller: RC520

Date: 13 Nov 2008


 

Symptom

EEMS NS84-01 encountered WAR0394 ionizer blower alarm, and after checking the wiring found that output shuttle ionizer fan no close loop although LED light is green.


 


 

Action

After replaced with a spare new fan from the old store, problem still persist.

New fan serial no. is above 01000, so is new type and able to support fan stop alarm also.

Old type serial below 01000 only can support fan cleaning alarm.

To activate this function, pls refer to the attached file. If your handler wiring is new type (5 and 8 close loop, 6 and 7 bypass), change the fan jumper setting to PIN2-3. Default jumper setting is PIN1-2, PIN3-4.

After change new jumper setting, no more alarm.

Refer Procedure for activate Cleanning alarm & Fan stop alarm  in KD750B.

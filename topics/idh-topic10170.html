Service History

Subject: Xilinx- NS8080 Load cell check

Handler Model: NS-8080

Controller: RC520

Date: 17 Jul 2009


 

Symptom

Unable to complete load cell check as reading on the logger is too low(2.5~3.5 kgf) compared to actual settings(5kgf).


 


 

Action

1. Try out 2 sets for Quad SLK for HMI setup of 5kgf device per device in NS-8080, found out pressure displayed in TEAC transducer indicator showing only 2.8kgf for Arm 1 and 3.1kgf for Arm 2. Another set of SLK also low pressure.

2. Verified Quad SLK in NS-7080W also pressure low, found out air leakage at compliance cylinder gasket areas. Inform to customer and was told to do checking again after they sent out their Quad SLK to K.A for servicing.


 

Using Xilinx's Teac load cell and quad layout kit, perform contact force check(quad site, enable site A).

When set for contact force for 5kgf in MMI Contact but load cell monitor should 2.9kf when in contact.

Need to use Load cell at getech office to confirm whether load cell AD converter have problem because this Teac load cell AD converter has not been calibrated for at least 5 years.


 

21 Jul 2009 NS-8080 (S/N: 181523)

1. Brought in 1 set of 80x60 SLK and 80x60 transducer plate from Getech Office.

2. Using Xilinx's load cell checker and transducer and carried out load cell check on NS-8080, but found out contact force pressure still low, 3.1kgf for both index arms. Suspect Xilinx's load cell checker may be due for calibration.

Remarks: Will bring in Getech's keyence load cell checker to check contact force again.


 


 

23 Jul 2009 NS-8080 (S/N: 181523)

1. Carried out load cell check with Getech's Keyence load cell checker and transducer.

2. Performed contact force check according to Xilinx's requirements of 5kgf, 10kgf and 20kgf per device.

3. Measured both Index arms' site A to D.

Remarks: Both index arms passed within spec of +/- 1.5kgf per site

Service History

Subject: NEC NS-7000 S/N: 181350 replacement of EP regulator and Shuttle 2 linear guide, bend lead

Handler Model: NS-7000 (S/N: 181350)

Controller: RC520

Date: 6 Sep 2010


 

Symptom

EP regulator reads 400kPa due to wrong air tubing connection. Requested by customer to change the new EP regulation. Removal of Shuttle 2 linear guide found that the slider plastic cover chipped off and some particles found on the linear guide. Linear guide replaced, teaching and running check carried out. Release to production and monitor for 1 hrs. During monitoring found site 3 always bin 2 on both index arms. Socket removed found the socket pins faulty and socket replaced but problem still persist. Tried to swap the socket connection at tester side found site 3 connector faulty. Therefore, site 3 connector replaced and testing back to bin 1.


 

Operator complained bend leads at shuttle 1. Shuttle 1 and index arm 1 teaching carried out and the bend leads reduced.


 


 

Action

Found contactor's nests faulty. Nests changed and bend leads solved.


 


 

Cause

-EP regulator reads 400kPa due to wrong air tubing connection

-Bend lead due to faulty contactor's nest.

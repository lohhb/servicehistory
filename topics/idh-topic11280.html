Service History

Subject: AMD NS3000 ATC NS303 Chiller over upper limit alarm

Handler Model: NS-3000 ATC

Controller: RC520

Date: 19 Nov 2009


 

Symptom

NS303 running temperature setpoint 35DegC, same for NS301 & NS302.

Chiller is Silicon Thermal CH1250 (M33, MD30).

Chiller water temperature goes up to upper limit of 40DegC and cause alarm.

Chiller setting:

- setpoint 20 DegC

-lower temp limit 10 DegC

-upper temp limit 40DegC


 

After NS303 running sometimes, chiller cannot stay at 20 DegC setpoint, it will slowly goes up to 40DegC and cause alarm.

Checked on NS301 & 302, the chiller temperature cannot be maintained, always fluctuate around 22 to 35 DegC.


 

Open chiller for NS303, and found one of the copper pipe joint got air leak, small air bubbles.

Unable to see any other abnormalities physically with the chiller. Water in chiller is flowing.


 


 

Action

11 Dec 2009

APP found compressor of chiller kick in and cut off during production.

This is not normal as it is supposed to be always ON.

Found compressor to be unusually HOT and the cut off is actually not temperature cool down but rather compressor overheat.

Measured current during compressor kick in to be about 14amps which is not normal as it is supposed to be only 2.5amps during running. This suggest overloading of compressor.

All 4 chillers having the same symptoms.

APP also suggested the possibility of supply 50Hz sufficient making the compressor to overwork.

KG(S) to decide the next course of action.


 

18 Dec 2009 email from APP Systems Services Pte Ltd

Silicon Thermal CH1250 (M33, MD30)M-33 with MD-30 pump is made to run at 115V/60 Hz power supply at AMD Singapore.

Running at 50 Hz will not be able to diagnose the chiller further.

In fact when we were at AMD recently, we have already done an off-load test on the chiller using the 50 Hz power supply over there and the same phenomenon was noticed.

So most likely the result will also be the same if the off-load test is done in our service centre. We do not have the 60 Hz power supply over here.


 


 

24 Jun 2010

New type of chiller NESLAB ThermoFlex recirculating chiller installed by APP at AMD on NS301.

No more over temperature alarm.


 

Cause

Old Silicon Thermal CH1250 (M33, MD30) chiller not suitable for use in AMD Singapore because the this chiller  runs at 50Hz where as the 230Vac power supply in Singapore is 60Hz.

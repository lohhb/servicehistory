Service History

Subject: EEMS NS8080-03 Drive Unit 1 Motor Driver (Servo Driver) 400W burned

Handler Model: NS-8080 (NS8080-03)

Controller: RC520

Date: 22 Mar 2010


 

Symptom

EEMS handler tripped over the weekend during production.


 


 

Action

They swapped NS8080-01 DU1 over temporary to continue running production on NS8080-03.

Check and found DU1 Motor Driver (Servo Driver) 400W burned.

Replaced with a servo driver 4 from Getech office, and swapped back to NS8080-03.

Also changed the new CT gain and offset in SPELCT.

Fix back original DU1 of NS8080-01 and performed dry cycle check. OK.

Raised warranty report S0413.

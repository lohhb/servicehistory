Service History

Subject: UTAC – NS-8040 ATC Thermo head slow in heating up

Handler Model: NS-8040 (S/N: 01

Controller: RC520

Date: 21 & 22 Mar 2011


 

Symptom

UTAC – NS8040 ATC Thermo head slow in heating up .


 


 

Action

21 Mar 2011

1)    Check chiller's settings ok.

2)    Check thermo controller's settings ok.

3)    Cleaned chiller's filter but still encountering slow in heating up.

4)    Physical check on thermo head ok.

5)    Verified using spare thermo head completed with connectors, water tubings, peltier, thermistor, heating up faster and power consumption around 80%(compare to existing at 100%)

6)   Temporary raise up chiller SP to 29ºC in order for thermo head to reach desire temperature.(S/R: 012226)
22 Mar 2011

7)    Measured Peltier TEC170 resistance around 9?(Spec 2 to 10?)

8)    Measured new Petier less than 5?.

9)    Existing Peltier on higher resistance causing slow in heating up.

10)  Replaced Peltier TEC 170

11)  Checked for water leakage.

12)  Bring down Chiller SP to 20ºC.

13)  Heat up thermo head to100ºC(faster than before replacement)

14)       Power consumption at around 80%. (S/R: 012227)

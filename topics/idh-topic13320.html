Service History

Subject: NS-7000 contactor "twist" during pick up from shuttle

Handler Model: NS-7000

Controller: RC520

Date: 8 Sep 2010


 

Symptom

SEP reported NS-7000 contactor "twist" during pick up from shuttle.


 


 

Action

When contactor blade is longer than guide pin, blade contact first resulting in loud contact noise. Need to run at low speed.

Found change kit not within EPSON design spec of shuttle thickness 12mm and guide pin 11mm.

In the case of thicker shuttle and longer guide pin, will hit chamber entry. When guide pin reduced length, result in contact blade loud noise.

SEP concern on alignment and small package handling for this long contact blade and short guide pin.

Contact head "twist" is actually feature of NS7000 series compliant unit. Ability to cater for slight offset during pick up.

Tried to re-teach P21, P22, P23, P24, P50 and P51, but both contactor ¡°twist¡± in different direction. Unable to offset of best fit and no ¡°twist.

SEP however request index arm alignment with alignment jig.


 

9 Sep 2010

Index Arm alignment carried out with alignment jig.

Found P50 and P51 y-pos difference of 1mm.

Audible and visible hard contact of contact blade and shuttle during pick up on index arm 1.

Teached P23 position. Difference of 1 mm.

No audible hard contact and minimal visible twist of contactor head.

Setup to 1x4. Running with dummies and alignment check.

Lead Inspection carried out on full tray pre/post scan. Pass

Running condition reverted to 80% speed with no loud contact sound as compared to previous running condition.


 


 

Cause

Change kit not within EPSON design spec of shuttle thickness 12mm and guide pin 11mm.

In the case of thicker shuttle and longer guide pin, will hit chamber entry. When guide pin reduced length, result in contact blade loud noise.

P23 for Input Shuttle 1 also out by 1 mm.

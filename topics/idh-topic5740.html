Service History

Subject: Cannot Pause and Start/Reset, need to Power On & Off

Handler Model: NS7080 (Xilinx, NS7K-04)

Controller: RC520

Date: 31 Jan 2008


 

Symptom

Handler cannot Pause and Start/Reset, need to Power Off and On the Handler.


 

Action

After some investigation, data-logger was by-passed and everything back to normal.

Observed another problem on the data-logger.

Rightfully, the Start of handler was forbidden when an error occurred.

Need to acknowledge the problem on the data-logger before resuming the handler.

But when the handler got problem, the operator box at rear position, the start at the operator was inactive (this is correct), but the start at the front panel is able to resume the handler without acknowledging the error on the data-logger.

Thus, I had told Alan to get his guys to re-look the wiring of data-logger and this wiring may have caused the above problem too.


 


 

Cause

Customer's data-logger with wrong wiring caused handler unable to Pause, Start/Reset.

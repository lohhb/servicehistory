Service History

Subject: NS-6040 Yamatake temperature controller board issue

Handler Model: NS-6040 (ASE)

Controller: SRC520

Date: 16~17 Dec 2008


 

Symptom

After replaced to new purchased board, customer auto tune board but reported temperature abnormal fluctuation on MMI display.


 


 

Action

Installed board to another NS-6040 and auto tune. Heat up to 70, 100 and 130 DegCels but MMI temperature display did not stablise. Temperature variation from setpoint temperature was about 1.4DegCels.

Other handlers shows temperature variation of 0.3DegCels from setpoint temperature.

Bring back board to office.


 

Test the board NS-6040 in office at  70, 90 and 130 DegCels with auto tuning.

Temperature variation from setpoint temperature was about 1.4DegCels.

It was still within EPSON spec., KG Winston Goh informed to return board to stock and replace ASE another board from stock.

Sent new board to ASE.


 

Replaced new Yamatake temperature controller board to ASE. Customer informed this new board is working fine.

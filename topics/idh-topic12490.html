Subject: NS-8080 Z Axis servo error

Handler Model: NS-8080 (NS8080-02) 8 Hands High UPH

Controller: RC520

Date: 5 May 2010


 

Email

Regarding Z-axis servo error, most of the errors happened after door open/door close. There maybe pick up errors or floating errors before operator open the door and tried to clear the jam and the Z-axis hand position may be accidentally shifted. So when you start again, Z-axis servo error will be detected.

 

The hands are controlled by servo which is different from the usual stepper motor on the NS-7000 and normal NS-8080. If the Z-axis hand position is shifted or moved, pause+reset+home will not be able to recover the error. You have to power on/off the operational panel fyi.

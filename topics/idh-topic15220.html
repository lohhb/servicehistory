Service History

Subject: Infineon – NS-004 socket temperature control error

Handler Model: NS-8040 (S/N: 181671)

Controller: RC520

Date: 11 Mar 2011


 

Symptom

Infineon – NS-004 socket temperature control error.


 


 

Action

Handler encounter Jam 1526 – CH9 temperature control error.

-Found Head 1 temp display 512 degree, resit pt sensor not ok, checked pt sensor resistance 110 ohms ok. Knock the pt sensor and the HMI display back to normal again.


 

-Heat up to 45 deg and 85 deg ok. When started to run dry cycle mode, socket temperature fluctuated and became 90+ deg.


 

-Checked socket pt sensor X229 connector under fix tray 1 and found red wire broken from molex pin. Temp display of socket became 512 deg.


 

-Measured resistance of socket pt sensor 110 ohms ok. No spare molex pin, therefore soldered back the wire to the old pin.


 

Heat up to 85 deg, run handler in dry cycle mode and release.

SR ref: 013158


 


 


 

Cause

Red wire broken for socket PT sensor X229 connector .

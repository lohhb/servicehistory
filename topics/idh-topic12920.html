Service History

Subject: NS-8080 Output arm unable to HOME. 2Ack error in Z-teach and "No information" HMI error.

Handler Model: NS-8080 (NS88-04, S/N: 181655) 8 Hands High UPH

Controller: RC520

Date: 7 Jun 2010


 

Symptom

EEMS NS-8080 Output arm unable to home and no torque for all 8 hands.


 


 

Action

-Swapped RS485-1 and RS485-2 connectors of RS485 board and used Z-teach to trigger homing for input arm ok, but still NG when trigger output arm, 2Ack error.

-Reload servo drivers for all 8 hands by Hidaka-san. NG.

-E-hand Z motion not smooth when power OFF and found small spring of spline shaft damaged. When power ON, can feel a bit vibration on the E-hand.

-Swapped Input arm B-hand over but problem persist. Can feel vibration but Z motion still smooth.

-Checked SPEL CT and found input bit #52 OTA HOMES not ON even though all hand in upper position.

-Check continuity of RS485-2 connector wiring(red, white, green) to CN5 on control board ok.

-Swapped IO ARM IN1 and IO ARM IN2 remote IO modules NG.

-Hidaka-san to request for spare parts from Japan for troubleshooting and replacement.


 


 

Cause


 

Remarks

Use multi-meter to check below when handler is off:

1. Between D-G  NO short-circuit.

(Resistance: >200 Ohm( Value will be depended on equipment))

2. Between 0-24V  NO short-circuit.

(Resistance: >few kOhm (Value will be depended on equipment))

Service History

Subject: NS-7000 Index Arm hang up after clean out

Handler Model: NS-7000 (EEMS, NS70-33, S/N 121203 & 181173)

Controller: RC520 (MMI 2.0h, SPEL CT 2.81b1h)

Date: 17 Jun 2008


 

Symptom

EEMS NS-7000 Index Arm hang up after clean out, MMI 2.0h.


 


 

Action

Installed the new patch MMI 2.0m for those index arm hang up machine with no problem so far and it is now still under monitoring.

EEMS is required to apply to the new patch of MMI 2.0m to all NS7000 handler.


 


 

Cause


 


 

Remarks

MMI 2.0i and 2.0j were reported to have software bug that caused Contact Counter kinds to auto reset at every Initial Start.

Refer 0064-NS7K and 0087-NS7K.

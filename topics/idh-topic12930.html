Service History

Subject: NS70-30 #3 ESD class 0 upgrade EP4 replacement

Handler Model: NS-7000 (NS70-30, S/N: 18996) UTAC

Controller: RC520 (S/N: 01-20285)

Date: 2 Jul 2010


 

Symptom

 Old EP4 used in March ion cannot balance.


 


 

Action

Replaced with the old EP4 used in March when ion cannot balance.

Swapped the white and the black color wires for the ION MONITOR port of EP4, ion able to balance. Previously for #4 upgrade, the 2 wires positions were different from this upgrade. #3 is black on top, white below but #4 is white on top, black below. Feedback to Hidaka san.

Also found ION24V1-1  0V  blue wire disconnected from the connector pin, repaired it OK.

Because the production need handler urgently, had to release without buying off.

Outstanding for this upgrade is ionizer and grounding checks buyoff, to be schedule by UTAC.

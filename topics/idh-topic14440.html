Subject: NS-7000 Open/close Hand Calibration


 

 

NS-7000 Open/close hand calibration is performed when 80mm open condition.

Open/Close teaching at 80mm pitch is equivalent to pulse 0 position.

 

1) Start SPEL CT


 

2) Input the following command on command execution window.

(The ">" sign indicates in Command Execution Window)

>SELRB 7     (Input arm open /close )  For Output arm case, SELRB 8

>MOTOR ON

>MCAL       <--  Open / Close hand at open condition.


 


 

3) Adjust Hand open pitch as 80mm.

   Shuttle C/K pocket edge to edge is useful. <-- Shuttle pocket pitch is just 80mm.

  (Use Hand Adjustment jig on shuttle)

>GO P*+X(1)    or      >GO P*-X(1)     <-- pitch Open/Close 1mm


 


 

4) The 80mm pitch condition, the pulse value is 0.

>calpls 0,0,0,0

>calib 1

>mcal

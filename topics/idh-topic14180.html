Service History

Subject: UTAC  NS-8080 8 Hands output arm z-axis servo error

Handler Model: NS-8080 (NS8080-05, S/N: 181725) 8 Hands High UPH

Controller: RC520

Date: 10~14 Jan 2011


 

Symptom

NS-8080 8 Hands always encounter z-axis servo error when press home or start. Unable to clear the alarm.


 


 

Action

-Swap rs485-1 cable to rs485 board rs485-2 port and used zteach to home input arm Ok. Used rs485-2 cable on rs485-1 port NG, output arm also cannot home. Confirmed rs485 board good.

-checked output arm driver and control board LED ok.

-Used zteach to home output arm no response and no error. But can feel vibration from hand A. All 8 hands no torque.

-Replaced driver module(driver board SKP480 + resolver board SKP481) ok. Spare parts received from Hidaka san.

-Dry cycle handler in 85/80% speed ok. Release handler.

Raise warranty S0465.


 

12 Jan 2011

Encountered same issue again.

Feedback to Hidaka san. He seek advise from Japan but Japan unable to advise next course of action.

As discussed and agreed by Hidaka san, Ray and myself, we replaced driver module(driver board SKP480 + resolver board SKP481) again while awaiting advise from Japan. Handler able to complete 1k qual lot.


 

13 Jan 2011

Driver board SKP480 spoilt again.

Received advise from Hidaka san in the afternoon Japan recommend to replace the z-axis module hand A also since can feel vibration when driver faulty.

To receive spare z-axis module on Fri 14/1/11 and replace together with a new driver board.


 

14 Jan 2011

Received z-axis module. Replaced z-axis module together with driver board SKP480 with resolver board SKP481. Dry cycle handler ok.

As informed by Ray, handler will continue dry cycle overnight

Service History

Subject: NS8080 Output arm Hand C unable to pickup devices and unable to home

Handler Model: ASE II – NS8080 S/N: 181416

Controller: RC520

Date: 15 Feb 2011 (3.5 Hrs)


 

Symptom

ASE II – NS8080 S/N: 181416 output arm Hand C unable to pickup devices and unable to home.


 


 

Action

Problem still persisted after verifying with another PG card.

Customer already verified with the stepper motor and driver card but still the same problem.

Checking of hofs and motor belt tension and condition.

Motor belt tension tried to adjust but still the same.

Tried resit all related connectors and problem solved.

Running check carried out for 1.5hrs without problem.


 


 

Cause

Electrical contact issue, because after resit connector of stepper motor for Output Hand C solved the problem.


 

Remarks

Dip switch on stepper driver if wrong setting will cause stepper motor to vibrate and unable to complete HOME or unable to complete Auto Hand Height calibration.

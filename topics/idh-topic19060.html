Subject: NS-8080 High UPH 8 Hands Autoteaching unable to complete at loader x-way

Handler Model: NS-8080 (S/N: 181655, H15-NS88)

Controller: RC520 (S/N: 03-02115)

Date: 14 Mar 2013


 

Symptom

After empty tray arm crashed with output arm issue.

H15-NS88(181655) Autoteaching unable to complete at loader x-way as per Choon Soon.


 


 

Action

Teaching sensor cannot sense out of the x-way black circle on the Loader jig.
-Check handler and handler able to complete auto teaching at loader x-way .
-Suspect it maybe due to teaching sensor connector interference with the input arm x-axis flat cable when it is teaching on the jig's x-way circle. And restricted the arm to move further.
-Tried auto teaching few times ok with consistent teaching point.
-Removed the 8-hand arm assembly inner covers as it has some restriction to the x-way flat cable at the output arm also.

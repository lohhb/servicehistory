Service History

Subject: NS-7000 Hotplate 1 overheating

Handler Model: NS-7000 (S/N: 18989)

Controller: RC520 (S/N: 01-20267)

Date: 20 Nov 2012


 

Symptom

UTAC NS-7000 (S/N: NS70-62) Hotplate 1 overheating issue cause BGA ball melt and meeting.


 


 

Action

1.Checked resistivity of thermo fuse.
2.Health check on hdd, clean up and error check.
3.Copy out whole NS7000 folder in c drive with observer data and passed to Saito san.
4.Encountered hotplate 2 when heat up, temp displayed 159deg. Checked hotplate 2 pt sensor and wiring, and displayed showed '---' dash. Then went back to normal temp display and heat up to 90deg hot mode. Feedback to Saito san.

Meeting on NS70-62 hotplate 1 overheating issue, NS7000 compliance 1x2 and 1x4 site default 40mm issue and NS7000 tray arrival no alarm issue.
1.NS70-62 hotplate 1 overheating issue. As above.
2.NS7000 compliance 1x2 and 1x4 site default 40mm issue. UTAC want software modified so that default is 55mm when selecting 1x2 and 1x4 sites.
3.NS7000 tray arrival issue. Tray arrival text settings in config default is 0. When tray arrival sensor cannot detect tray at Auto 1/2/3, handler will alarm but no option for retry. When set to '1', ok.

NS70-62 hotplate 1 device ball melted.

Encountered temperature error on hotplate 1 and temperature display showed “---“. After retry, temperature displayed 200-300deg.

According to EPSON, thermo fuse is for handler temperature safety, not for heater. Spec of fuse is that it will cut off at 196deg in hot oil. So even if temperature of heater is same as hot oil, fuse may not cut. It MAY cut at 220-240deg. For the temperature display at “---“, EPSON said the pt sensor measured it at less then 10deg with wrong signal. And heater continue to heat up without cut off.

Therefore, to counter this issue, they are researching on the following.

1)Pt sensor connector pin need to be examined, Sn plating –> Au plating.

 2)When MMi temperature display “---“, software need to cut off temperature control to prevent it from heating continuously.


 


 

Modified Sw for NS8080 sent by netporter to UTAC on 26th Dec. NS7000 sw will be ready by 14th Jan 2013 after NS8080 sw ok. But received ver. is 12.14 for NS8080 now, previous ver. was 2.2. To confirm whether ver. Is correct for NS8080.


 

For connector pins, Sn plating reliability is same as Au plating. Therefore Epson want to change only Pin for Pt sensor of hotplate on 2 handlers which had ball melting issue, and analyze it. Pin to be sent from 28th Dec onwards and Saito san to arrange schedule for replacement.

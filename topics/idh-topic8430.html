Service History

Subject: NS-8040 Device fly away after unload to output shuttles

Handler Model: NS-8040 (NS-100, S/N: 181213)

Controller: RC520

Date: 2~3 Apr 2009


 

Symptom

Infineon NS-100

(V93K)_WFWLB217-1_Q_AMB (device size 8x8x0.725) fly away after unload to output shuttles.


 

Action

Found Index Arm 1 & 2 purge air strength uneven.

Adjusted purge air volume.

Set HMI Index Counter Air On time to 0.3sec.

Auto Contact height.

Offline run with 70 pieces of device of glossy surface and fine tunning.

Online run with engineering sample devices but some devices flipped.

Index Arm 2-A consecutive 3 devices flipped, swapped contactor and flipped occurred at Index 2-D.

Removed rubber suction cup from Index 2-D and run, ok.

Informed KA to send new rubber suction cups for replacement.


 

KA replaced new suction cups.

Index Arm 2 first time unload devices to output shuttle 2, device flipped for pocket 2-A and 2-D, continue production run, 2444 pcs tested.

2nd lot 696 pcs, output shuttle pocket 1-A device did not sit properly in output shuttle 1 pocket.

Continue with production monitoring.


 


 

Cause

Uneven purge air strength.

Sticky rubber cups

For this change kit, after AutoContact Height, need to raise up contact height by 0.5mm to 3mm higher.

Shuttles' pocket depth is very shallow. eg. WFWLB-183 / 217

Subject: EEMS - Installation of index arm 1 and output arm pulley.

Handler Model: NS-7000(NS70-14, S/N: 181143)

Controller: RC520

Date: 26 Mar 2009


 

Symptom

Handler NS70-14 Index Am 1 crash.


 


 

Action

1) Installation of Index Arm 1 and Output Arm pulley completed.

2) Teaching of Index arm 1 & 2 and Shuttle 1 & 2 carried out after installation of index arm 1.

3) Calibration of output arm Y-axis motor carried out after installation of pulley.

4) Auto teaching carried out on shuttle 1 & 2, Auto 1-3 and Fix 1-3 using Auto-Teaching jig.

5) Alignment check carried out using shuttle alignment jig.

6) Dry run carried out at 80% speed.


 


 

Cause

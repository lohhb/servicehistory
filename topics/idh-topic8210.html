Service History

Subject: Auto Contact Height - Index Out of operating range

Handler Model: NS-7000

Controller:

Date:


 

Symptom

NS-7000 index arm required teaching of y-axis.

After teaching, HOME ok. But during contact height calibration, error index out of operating range.


 


 

Action

11 Feb 2009

NS-7000 index arm required teaching of y-axis.

After teaching, HOME ok. But during contact height calibration, error index out of operating range.

Found z, u axis points to be -110,0 and 0,-110 respectively for P50 and P51.

Advise customer to perform teaching for z and u axis.

Handler P50 and P51 z and u axis must be in -96mm range.


 

12 Feb 2009

After P50 and P51 teaching, no more error.

During RETRY+START Auto Contact Height calibration mode, unable to pickup device from Input shuttle.

Production can resume with normal manual offset adjustment of Contact Height.

Handler is running rasco pitch thus the reference pick up height from shuttle is important.

Advise customer to get reference pick up height from engineering group as data was given to engineering group during rasco pitch discussion.

Subject: Xilinx NS-7080W Automat's Transverser crashed

Handler Model: NS-7080W

Controller: RC520

Date: 9~10 Jan 2012


 

Symptom

Phone support – Xilinx NS-7080W Automat's Transverser (Robot 10 ) crashed.


 


 

Action

Advise customer to:

- calibrate Transverser Robot 10 Axis 2 at pulse 0 (hard stop position in Automat)

- for Transverse Robot 10 teach P1 & P2 position

During Homing, “Elevator interlock error, Elevator try to move while transverser was not at the correct position”.

(Elevator try to move while transverser was not at the correct position).

- advise to check Home sensor plate of Transverse to cut Transverse's Home sensor in Automat when Transverse in Automat

- calibrate Transverser Robot 10 Axis 2 at pulse 0 (hard stop position in Automat)

- for Transverse Robot 10 teach P1 & P2 position problem solved

After recalibrate pulse 0, teach P1 & P2, interlock problem solved.

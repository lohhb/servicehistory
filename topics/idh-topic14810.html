Service History

Subject: NS7000 F5018 – Absolute encoder battery alarm DU 2

Handler Model: NS-7000(S/N: 18732)

Controller: RC520

Date: 24 Feb 2011


 

Symptom

Silab – NS7000(18732) F-5018  Absolute encoder battery alarm  for Drive Unit 2.


 


 

Action

Check old battery in existing Drive Unit 2, measured 1.29V.

Checked new battery 3.7V, replaced.

Power up, restart SPEL CT, home handler and cycle run ok.

SR no:013157


 


 

Cause

Low voltage of Lithium Battery caused Absolute Encoder alarm.

Lithium Battery - R35N700002100

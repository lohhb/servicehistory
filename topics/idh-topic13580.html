Subject: IDT NS-7000 loader tray lock able to lock but unable to unlock

Handler Model: NS-7000

Controller: RC520

Date: 6 Jan 2011


 

Symptom

IDT NS-7000 loader tray lock able to lock but unable to unlock.


 


 

Action

Swapped with Auto3 tray lock and same, able to lock and unable to unlock.

I/O monitor same. Able to lock but unable to unlock.

SPEL I/O same. Able to lock but unable to unlock.

But Auto1 and Auto2 able to lock and unlock.

Advised chuah to swap Auto1 or Auto2 tray lock as he already tried with Auto3.

After swapped, able to lock and unlock. Suspect Loader and Auto3 cylinder faulty thus able to lock but unable to unlock.


 


 

Cause

Faulty Loader and Auto3 cylinder faulty, able to lock but unable to unlock.

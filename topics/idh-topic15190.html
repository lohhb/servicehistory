Service History

Subject: NS-8080 High UPH Input Hand F and H vibrating

Handler Model: UTAC NS-8080 (NS8080-01, S/N: 181665)

Controller: RC520

Date: 3, 9 & 11 Mar 2011


 

Symptom

UTAC – NS8080-01 input hand F and H vibrating.


 


 

Action

3 Mar 2011

Input hand F and H vibrating at down position which is pickup at loader and placement at shuttle. Took video and send to Hidaka san.

Awaiting advise to change driver module or driver module together with hands.


 

9 Mar 2011

As per Hidaka san request,

1)checked input arm hand F and H motor temperature by feeling and compare to the rest of the hands, no difference.

2)Power off panel and feel z movement of hand F and H, NG. But twist and turn the hand tip a bit and z movement smooth again.

3)Upgrade HMI from v1.8 to v1.9 and check when hand up sensor error occurs, no RS485 unknown error. But version displayed in observer is still 1.8, so need to change the version in log folder product.dat to 1.9.

4)collect latest observer data.


 

11 Mar 2011

Collect and send to Hidaka san for 8-hand input arm hand F and H vibrating issue.

Service History

Subject: SCS  NS-8080 S/N:181634 abnormal temperature offset

Handler Model: NS-8080 (S/N:181634)

Controller: RC520

Date: 5 Oct 2009


 

Symptom

NS-8080 Socket offset values were too high compared to other 3 systems.


 


 

Action

Found that the socket base plate not level due to Socket adaptor block.

Spacers were added to solve the leveling issue.

Temp cal redo for socket and chamber on 70, 100 and 130 degree.

Offset values ok.


 


 

Cause

Socket base plate not level due to Socket adaptor block causing high temperature offset.

Subject: UTAC - NS70-27 KD-750B ionizer upgrade

Handler Model: NS-7000

Controller: RC520

Date: 21 Apr 2009


 

Symptom

NS70-27 KD-750B ionizer upgrade.


 

Action

Removed 2x KD-730B ionizer and install 5x KD750B ionizer fans with 3x power adaptor at loader, input shuttle, output shuttle, fix tray and unloader positions, including laying of close loop/grounding wires.

Check close loop functionality of each ionizer to confirm fan stop alarm.

Perform ionizer functionality check with ION system model 280 measuring equipment.

Total 21 zones within UTAC spec: decay time from 1000v to 35v under 5secs for positive and negative charge,

balance voltage not more than 35 v for positive and not less than -35 for negative charge.

Service History

Subject: NS-6040 Output Shuttles missing/flying devices in Hot mode

Handler Model: NS-6040(S/N: 15456)

Controller: RC520

Date: 19~22 Jun 2009


 

Symptom

During buyoff, Hot mode alignment check completed with lot of missing devices at both shuttle 1 and 2. Found that one of the nest faulty, adjusting of counter air, counter air time and release height but problem still persist. Still have missing/flying devices


 


 

Action

a) Hot mode devices flying solved after adjusting individual counter air.


 

b) During confirmation run, shuttle 1 device flipped. Shuttles speed reduced to 70% but problem still occurred. Suspect the roller guide issue found a thick layer of dirt accumulated. After some cleaning and greasing, the issue  frequency reduces.


 

other issues:

Running of QFP 14x14, devices dropped at index arm occurred very frequent. Found that the devices were given to me wrongly.

c) After changing another tray of QFP 14x14 devices, devices dropped at index arm occurred less frequent but a few case of false errors on the device dropped and vacuum error.

After adjusting of vacuum sensitivity issue solved.

d) Empty Tray arm unable to pickup 14x14 empty tray. Found that the force of the tray arm was too strong when picking up empty tray therefore, the air valve was adjusted and also the tray pad found tilted.


 


 

Cause

Counter air at Index Arm too strong cause Output Shuttles missing/flying devices in Hot mode.

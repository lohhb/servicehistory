Service History

Subject: Unable to execute Auto Contact Height and

Handler Model: NS-6040 (UTAC, NS60-21T)

Controller: SRC-520CE

Date: 31 Dec 2007


 

Symptom

NS6040 encounter homing problem with home sensor off error.

Output arm Hand 1 & 2 unable to mcal or Home.


 

Action

Found out rear of SRC520, both expansion I/O 1 and 2 connectors swapped by UTAC member who previously open up SRC520 and repair cooling fan.

Swapped back both Expansion I/O 1 and 2 connectors, O.K


 


 

Cause

Found out that the I/O connector I/O 1 and I/O 2 connectors were connected wrongly behind the SRC520CE controller.


 


 

Remarks

Same situation as 0002-NS6K.

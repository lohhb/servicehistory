Subject: Maybach 10x10 ATC Thermal Head/ Change-kit setup in NS-8160W 8IO Handler

Handler Model: NS-8160W High UPH 8 hands

Controller: RC520

Date: 03 Jun 2013(Mon) - 07 Jun 2013(Fri) 40 Hrs


 

Symptom

Advantest, Germany
Maybach 10x10 ATC Thermal Head/ Change-kit setup in NS-8160W 8IO Handler.


 


 

Action

1) Physically check the condition on single force thermal head and changekit - OK
2) Physically check new tray for Maybach device OK (ePak X=8div,Y=23div)
3) De-installed Tequila thermal head from index arm 1.
4) Installed single force thermal head onto index arm 1 and changekit on handler for verification.
5) Created setupfile for new Maybach device.
6) Performed auto-contact height calibration with and without device -OK
7) Installed new PnP suction cups.
8) Performed pick and place of device on handler.
9) Performed check with tester on Maybach Setup - OK.
10) Conduct maintenance training with customer on hard-disk recovery, backup and restore handler data.
11) Tried out Dual force Maybach Thermal head on Arm 2, found index arm exert too much force on input shuttle during performing auto contact height calibration with 0 PSI additional force.
12) Tried out 2-5 PSI additional force, conditon same. Customer choose not to use dual force thermal head.
13) Requested by customer to install Tequila thermal head on index arm 2 and Maybach thermal head on index arm 1, reason to minimize damage on thermal head when install/ de-install thermal head in/ out of handler.
14) Cycling check with device for both Tequila and Maybach setup.
15) Performed check with Tester on Tequila Setup- OK
16) Inform customer to check on HMI setting when either select Maybach or Tequila setup.

Subject: NS-8080 High UPH 8 Hands Output Arm unable to Home

Handler Model: NS-8080 High UPH 8 Hands (S/N: 182120)

Controller: RC520 (S/N: 03-30057)

Date: 18 Jun 2012


 

Symptom

18 Jun 2012: ASE – H24-NS88 output arm unable to home.


 


 

Action

Output arm hand A 2Ack error, hand C vibrate.
Replaced Output Hand A/C driver module, hand C can home and no vibration. Hand A a lot vibration.
Replaced Output hand A.
Checked vacuum and purge air.
Perform master calibration and aligned Output hand A pitch.
Dry cycle run ok and release.
Ref: warranty S0518.


 


 

Cause

Output Hand A spoilt.

Output A/C Driver board and resolver board spoilt.

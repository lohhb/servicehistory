Service History

Subject: NS-8040 Encountered Blower ionizer cleaning warning and The RAS circuit detected control unit malfunction

Handler Model: NS-8040 (NS80-26)

Controller: RC520

Date: 16 Mar 2010


 

Symptom

NS-8040 Encountered Blower ionizer cleaning warning and The RAS circuit detected control unit malfunction.


 


 

Action

Home and Start handler, encountered "Blower ionizer cleaning warning" due to missing 1 KD-750B ionizer above input shuttle.

Following encountered "The RAS circuit detected control unit malfunction".

Tried re-sit MIB board, connectors for DU slot 1 and reboot handler a few times.

Error disappear- problem still unknown.


 


 

Cause

Missing KD-750 blower ionizer caused  "Blower ionizer cleaning warning".

"The RAS circuit detected control unit malfunction" maybe due to connection problems(eg. MIB, connectors to MIB and DU 1).


 


 

Remarks

Same "The RAS circuit detected control unit malfunction" error in 0170-NS8K.

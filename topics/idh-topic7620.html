Service History

Subject: EEMS NS7000 Output arm crash

Handler Model: NS-7000

Controller: RC520

Date: 29 Aug 2008


 

Symptom

EEMS NS7000 Output arm crash.


 


 

Action

Output am unit removed from NS70-02W to service NS70-10.

Absolute encoder backup error occurred at Input & Output arm during homing of handler. Resetting of Input & Output arm encoder in Drive Unit 2 solved the problems.

During alignment check, 80mm x-pitch and hand C & D adjusted.

Auto teaching and dummy run carried out on Auto 1-3 and Fix 1-3.

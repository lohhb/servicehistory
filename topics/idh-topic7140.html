Subject: AMD NS303 Recovery CD(EasyRestore) error when using Samsung 2.5" 80GB HDD

Handler Model: UBS

Controller: RC520

Date: 1 Sep 2009


 

Symptom

AMD NS303 Recovery CD(EasyRestore) error when using Samsung 2.5" 80GB HDD.

Error 1527 "bad update sequence number" during recovery at 77% using recovery CD on Samsung 2.5" 80GB HDD.

Click 3 times ok and exit to DOS.

Try to do recovery again but still same error.


 


 

Action

Use Hitachi 2.5" 40GB with NS-3000 Recovery, able to complete recovery process to HDD.


 

Cause

Samsung 80GB HDD not compatible with NS-3000 Recovery CD.

It could be that Recovery software by EasyRestore is old that does not support large capacity HDD. EasyRestore may not support HDD physical capacity larger than 40GB.

HDD partitioning may not work.

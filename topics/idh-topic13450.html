Re-teach P51 teaching point, Calibration on Shuttle 1 Pulse 0 Position. And also re-teaching the Shuttle 1 P21 & P23 point.

  Re-align Pick and Place offset Alignment for Shuttle 1. Dummy device run for 1 tray.

  When running actual devices, encounter Bin 2 & 5 error. Technician suspect device problem.

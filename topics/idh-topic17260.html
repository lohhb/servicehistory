Service History

Subject: NS-7080W Input Shuttle 1 unable to heat up

Handler Model: NS-7080W (S/N: 121298, NS7KW-07)

Controller: RC520 (S/N: 01-25326)

Date: 29 Aug 2011


 

Symptom

29 Aug 2011: Xilinx – NS7KW-07(121298) input shuttle 1 unable to heat up


 


 

Action

-Measured resistivity for input shuttle 1 heater plate 200V supply, no resistance; open.
-Checked input shuttle 1 heater plate 192 deg thermo fuse, no resistance; open.
-Replaced 192 deg thermo fuse with xilinx spare.
-Heat up to sp 85 deg, temperature stable.
SR: 013172


 


 

Cause

Thermo fuse blown for Input Shuttle 1.

Service History

Subject: During buyoff,

1) Flying devices in test site

2) Empty tray arm wobble a lot

3) Device place problem at unloader

Handler Model: NS-6040 (S/N: 15477)

Controller: RC520

Date: 1~3 Jul 2009


 

Symptom

During buyoff,

1) Flying devices in test site

2) Empty tray arm wobble a lot

3) Device place problem at unloader


 


 

Action

1) Flying devices in test site

- all individual counter air at index arm 1 and 2 was re-adjusted and device was run again to monitor situation


 


 

2) Empty tray arm wobble a lot

- screw unable to tighten at all, thus got a a lot of free movement.  Replaced a new empty tray arm and problem is dissolved.


 


 

3) Device place problem at unloader

- found out that all problem is linked to loader, that is, after "END OF TRAY" at loader, when empty tray arm pick tray from loader to empty tray buffer, it is more on "-y'.

It was concluded that the tray stopper at loader was being adjusted previously.  Re-adjusted and teaching carried out without misplacement at unloader.


 

others:

7~8 Jul 2009

Hot jam fine tuning carried out at 50 deg as recommended by Wee Boon.

Upon ramming up to 130 deg, a lot of flying devices at index arm 2, thus all manifold at arm 2 were changed.

After manifold changed, converted to 14x14 to run RVSI.

Note: 5x5 change kit was handed over to Kuo Hong to do ambient jam rate

Part: Vacuum unit for index (Vacuum Pump)

    Spec: ZX100-Q45MO-PBC-X125 (new spec: ZX100-QU5MO-PBC-X125)

    Code: R33ZT96155900

    Qty: 05


 


 

9~10 Jul 2009

RVSI carried out and fine tuning carried out for all unloaders (14x14 QFP).

PM carried out to cur the squeaking noise from index.  While doing PM, it was found out that one of the filter screw broke off. Vacuum Pump Filter screw replaced.


 

14~17 Jul 2009 Flying devices at output shuttle 2 for site 3

While doing  tuning, there is always flying device on index arm 2 site 3.

As all manifolds on index arm 2 were new, the first suspect was contactor.

Counter air was on manually and it was found out that the plunger did not protrude out when counter air activated.

Thus, contactor taken out for PM and 'O' ring was replaced as the most probable cause.

Tuning continued without this error anymore.

Drifting observed on loader 'Y' way, but when happened, will affect maybe a row on hot plate or a few pieces (sometimes on +y and sometimes -y).


 


 

Cause


 

Remarks

For flying device after release from Index Arm to Output Shuttle, check points:

1) Redo Auto Contact Height (after AutoContact Height increase the device release height by 0.5mm to 3mm)

2) Index Nest (worn out? such as chip off?)

3) O-ring worn out on plunger (harden? broken?)

4) Plunger in the contactor(dirty? plunger able to spring out?)

5) Spring in Contactor (is it dirty? in good condition?)

6) Rubber cup in Contactor (sticky? that stick device? harden? worn out?)

7) Index Counter Air On Time (increase or decrease?)

8) For 1x2 or inline4, Output Shuttle Device Detection sensor(horizontal) alignment & working condition

9) Index Counter Air (purge air) volume eg. is it too strong or weak

Subject: XAP NS7KW-06 Device drop and pickup error for Index Arm 1

Handler Model: NS-7080W(NS7KW-06, S/N: 121040)

Controller: RC520

Date: 22 & 23, 27 Apr 2010


 

Symptom

Device drop/pick up error Index Arm 1.

Device scratch from Index Arm 1.


 


 

Action

Found P51, Y needs reteach of +0.8mm.

Found vacuum sensor on during pick up but immediately off and device unable to pick up after up motion from input shuttle.

Reduce 50% speed @ ambient mode running dummy units.

Will verify index arm alignment again.

Meeting, please refer to minutes.


 

Device dropped in Hot mode.

Checked planarity and parallelism,

Used shim temporary for planarity as handler will be going to japan.

Adjust P51 to accommodate the best of Arm 1 and Arm 2 for parallelism.

Dummy units running, 70% speed, ok.

Handler pre-check for parts to be replaced in Japan.


 

27 Apr 2010

5) Checked connection plate ok.

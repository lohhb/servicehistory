Service History

Subject: Blue screen showing "Stop error" whenever power on the handler

Handler Model: NS-7000

Controller: RC520

Date: 22~23 Oct 2008


 

Symptom

The Handler S/N:18825 which was located at Getech's office found to have a faulty 2.5" HDD during the power on of the handler. A number of blue screen showing "Stop error" whenever power on the handler.


 


 

Action

After changing of HDD and did a recovery, the problem still the same.

Changed the CPU board and installed back the original HDD, the problem solved.


 


 


 

Cause

Faulty CPU board.

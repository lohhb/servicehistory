Service History

Subject: UTAC- NS70-76 output shuttle 1 and 2 device misplacement

Handler Model: NS-7000

Controller: RC520

Date: 27 Jun 2009


 

Symptom

Output Shuttle 1 and 2 Device misplacement.


 


 

Action

1. Check and reduce index arm counter air.

2. Increase release height by 0.1mm.

3. Reduce shuttle speed from 50% to 35%.

4. Remove tape pasted on output shuttle grooves.

5. Enabled chamber fan in ambient mode.

Encountered 1 jam in an hour.


 


 


 

Cause

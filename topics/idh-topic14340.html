Service History

Subject: Software development for NS-6000, NS-7000 and NS-8000 using Recovery CD issue

Handler Model: NS-6000/NS-7000/NS-8000

Controller: RC520

Date: 6 Jan 2011


 

Symptom

Same CU with 2 different HDD. 1 HDD for NS7000/NS8000 and 1 HDD for NS6000.

Purpose of software development/modification for customers by ESP.

Unable to recover/install NS6000 on HDD.


 


 

Action

Recovery CD by Ghost version+6941, blue screen. Unable to boot up.

Recovery CD by Easy Restore+6941, blue screen. Unable to boot up.

Manual recovery by OS installation, adding of drivers and SPEL resources, SPEL s/w and MMI s/w installation.

Unable to initialize SPEL.

Found CPU board is for NS8000 with 6axis MIB board.


 


 

Cause

Hardware compatibility different from recovery CD thus explaining recovery successful but failure to boot up.

Subject: NS-7080W Device stuck in socket and 100+ units has damage ball

Handler Model: NS-7080W(NS7KW-08)

Controller: RC520

Date: 10 Nov 2010


 

Symptom

NS-7080W Device stuck in socket and 100+ units has damage ball.

Index Arm 1 pick up error, disable and 1 arm in production.

Error log no indication of device drop.


 


 

Action

Suspect maybe due to mis-operation, device pick up error has SKIP+START as remedy. Operator did not remove device/did not check socket clear.

Index Arm 1 slider loose.

Whole index arm excessive play up/down.

Tightened screws and no play.

Dummy run with units 2000, OK, no pick up error from Index Arm 1.

Enable both arms and release to production.


 


 

Cause

Index Arm 1 slider loose caused excessive play for up/down, resulted in damage to BGA ball.
